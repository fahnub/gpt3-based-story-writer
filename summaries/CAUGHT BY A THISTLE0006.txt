Peppery Polly's people, Freddie Firefly, grew discouraged and reflected that there were two things he could do. He could go back alone to the clover field and try to set that ill-tempered worker free--and no doubt get stung by her for his pains. Or he could go to the dance of the Fireflies over near the swamp, and have a delightful time. Freddie decided to go to the dance.
