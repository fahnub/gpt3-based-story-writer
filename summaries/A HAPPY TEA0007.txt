Sancho, a dog, sits up and touches his paw to his head, as if he is taking off his hat to salute. Thorny laughs in spite of himself, and Miss Celia, seeing that the ice is broken, seats the little girls on one side, Ben and the dog on the other, and takes the head herself. Bab and Betty soon start chatting away to their pleasant hostess as if they have known her for a long time.
