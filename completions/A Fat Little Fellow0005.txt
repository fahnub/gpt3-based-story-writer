Meadow Mouse didn't want to move.  "The grass will grow again," he explained. "Farmer Green can't stop the  grass from growing, no matter how often he cuts it." And of course that  was quite true.  After haying Master Meadow Mouse had to be more careful than ever. He  knew that the hawks would scan the meadow many times a day in hopes of  catching a glimpse of his reddish-brown back.
