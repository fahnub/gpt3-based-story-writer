Rabbit, de pleasure wuz all his, suh  De ridin' wuz easy ez eatin' tater   When it's b'iled an' made inter pie!  Kaze under bofe wings he had a paw, suh,  An', when Brer Buzzard try fer ter drap 'im.   He'd scratch an' tickle 'im wid his claw, suh;  An' when Brer Buzzard try fer ter flap 'im,   He'd scratch an' wink his eye!
