"If the villagers can't beat your eggs they certainly can't use them for  omelettes," Polly Plymouth Rock told Henrietta one day. "Everybody knows  you have to beat eggs to make an omelette."  Henrietta Hen didn't know what to say to that. It was almost the only  time she was ever known to be silent.
