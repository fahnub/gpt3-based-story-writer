Somewhere he could hear a loud buzz, as of several angry voices. But he  did not care to show himself enough to find out what was happening. For  the time being he was content to stay snugly hidden among the thick  clover leaves.  [Illustration: Buster Shouted For Everybody to Keep Quiet. (_Page 48_)]  After a while the uproar ceased.
