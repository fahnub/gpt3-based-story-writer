Deer Mouse was only too glad to accept the invitation. And he  waited politely until Fatty had reached the ground, before going down  himself.  Old Mr. Crow saw them the moment they entered the cornfield. And he  hurried up to them with a most important air and advised them both that  they "had come to a dangerous place."
