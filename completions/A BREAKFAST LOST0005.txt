And without thanking him for his explanation (for she was  still angry) she turned and went off towards the meadow.  The whole affair amused Mr. Crow greatly. It kept him in a good humor  all that day. And he went about telling everybody how Grandfather Mole  had dug himself out of sight in the garden, almost under the cat's nose.  For that was exactly what had happened.
