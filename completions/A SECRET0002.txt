You'll have to bring it here, for I haven't any time to waste  to-day."  Miss Kitty Cat felt somewhat hurt. She went out and sat on the doorstep  and looked in at Farmer Green's wife. Now and then she gave a plaintive  mew. But Mrs. Green wouldn't pay any attention to her.  "Dear me!" said Miss Kitty. "Mrs. Green is busier than ever this  morning."
