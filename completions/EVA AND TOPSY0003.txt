I know it quite well, and I am going soon.'  'And I want to go,' she went on, 'only I don't want to leave you--it almost breaks my heart.'  'Don't, Eva, don't talk so. What makes you so sad?'  'I feel sad for our poor people. I wish, papa, they were all free. Isn't there any way to have all slaves made free?'
