Well, the Story Girl was destined to stand before kings herself, and she  was to be one whom they delighted to honour. But we did not know  that, as we sat in the old orchard. We thought it quite sufficiently  marvellous that she should expect to have the chance of just seeing  them.  "Can a queen do exactly as she pleases?"
