"I had a perfectly awful dream last night," said Cecily, with a shudder  of remembrance. "I dreamed I was on a desert island inhabited by tigers  and natives with two heads."  "Oh!" the Story Girl looked at Cecily half reproachfully. "Why couldn't  you tell it better than that?
