"Now I'll show you my toys, and we'll have a little play before it gets  too late for Thorny to stay with us," said Miss Celia, as Randa carried  away the tea-things and brought back a large tray full of picture-books,  dissected maps, puzzles, games, and several pretty models of animals,  the whole crowned with a large doll dressed as a baby.  At sight of that, Betty stretched out her arms to receive it with a cry  of delight.
