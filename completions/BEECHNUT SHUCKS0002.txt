Old Mr. Crow _haw-hawed_.  "I don't believe," he croaked, "you've ever met a cat."  "No, I haven't," Frisky Squirrel replied, "but I'd like to see one. So  I'll be on my way. But don't worry, Mr. Crow? I won't hurt her." And  then Frisky started off along the top of the stone wall once more, at a  somewhat brisker pace to make up for lost time.  "He can't say I didn't warn him," Mr. Crow exclaimed as he watched the  bouncing bit of gray fur.  "I hope Mr. Crow won't worry," said Frisky Squirrel
