After having somewhat recovered from the shock, she picked up the key, locked the door, and went upstairs into her chamber to compose herself; but she could not rest, so much was she frightened.  Having observed that the key of the closet was stained, she tried two or three times to wipe off the stain, but the stain would not come out.
