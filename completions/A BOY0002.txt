They had their play-time first; and, while the boys waited  inside, they heard the shouts of the girls, the banging of the wood, and  the final crash, as the well-packed pile went down. Then, as the lassies  came in, rosy, breathless, and triumphant, the lads rushed out to man  the breach, and labor gallantly till all was as tight as hard blows  could make it.
