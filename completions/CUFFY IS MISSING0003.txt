He felt that he ought to go for  help, somewhere. But he had promised Cuffy Bear to wait for him.  Then all at once an idea came to him. Why not send Peter Mink for help?  "Won't you please go down to Cedar Swamp and ask Fatty Coon to come up  here?" Nimble begged Peter.  "I can't," Peter answered. "I must go home now."
