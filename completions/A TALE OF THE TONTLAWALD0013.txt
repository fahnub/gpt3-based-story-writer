But I hope you will stay here always, as I can't bear you to go away. Have you ever been on the sea?'  'The sea?' asked Elsa, staring; 'what is that? I've never heard of such a thing!'  'Oh, I'll soon show you,' answered the girl, taking the lid from the box, and at the very bottom lay a scrap of a cloak, a mussel shell, and two fish scales. Two drops of water were glistening on the cloak, and these the girl shook on the ground. In an instant the garden and lawn and everything else had vanished
