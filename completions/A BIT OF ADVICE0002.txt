"Something seems to pull me right away from it," Dickie Deer Mouse told  him.  Fatty Coon groaned.  "Something seems to pull me _towards_ the corn!" he wailed.  Dickie Deer Mouse couldn't help feeling sorry for him.  "If there was only something else that you liked better than green  corn," he said, "perhaps it would help you to keep away from this new  danger."  "But there isn't!" Fatty Coon exclaimed.  "Have you ever tried _horns_?" Dickie Deer Mouse asked him.  Fatty Coon looked puzzled.
