Sancho was in great spirits, evidently  feeling that their woes were over and his foraging expeditions at an  end, for he frisked about his master with yelps of pleasure, or made  playful darts at the ankles of his benefactress, which caused her to  cry, "Whish!" and "Scat!" and shake her skirts at him as if he were a  cat or hen.
