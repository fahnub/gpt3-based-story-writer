So he began to paw away the soft heavy  snow, which wasn't more than a foot deep; and he was enjoying a good  meal when he heard a sudden _woof_ behind him.  Nimble wheeled instantly. And there, at the mouth of the cave, peering  over the tangle which screened it, Cuffy Bear stood upon his hind legs,  rubbing his eyes. Catching sight of Nimble, Cuffy blinked at him.  "Where's Nimble Deer, madam?" Cuffy Bear growled presently.  "I'm right here!"
