But you don't need to come any nearer,"he added.  Benny Badger stopped and squatted in the grass. He was glad to rest, forhe was--as has been said--no great traveller.  "Is anybody at home?" he asked presently.  "Somebody is," said the owl.  "Then I'll dig right in as soon as I get my breath," said Benny
