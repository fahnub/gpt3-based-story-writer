It was a good home. But it had one drawback. If anybody came along when  its owner was asleep in it--Well, Master Meadow Mouse didn't like to  think about that. The little nest at the end of the tunnel where he had  once lived had been far safer.  "I know what I'll do!" he cried at last, as a happy thought came to him.  "I'll hang a sign outside my door."
