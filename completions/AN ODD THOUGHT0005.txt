"You can't fool me,"  Grunty declared. "You have insulted my whole family. And it's time that  you learned better manners. I see that I shall have to teach you a  lesson."  Well, when they heard that speech Jolly Robin and his wife had to laugh.  The idea of a lesson in manners from Grunty Pig was the funniest thing  on the farm.
