When Dickie Deer Mouse awoke, after his first sleep in the underground  chamber, he thought that summer had come. He hadn't felt so comfortable  for weeks. And for a little time he lay quite still, half dozing,  enjoying the delightful warmth.  And then all at once he came to his senses.
