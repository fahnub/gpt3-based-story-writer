"Maybe I'm  not enough of a dandy--"  Just then somebody interrupted him. It was Henrietta Hen. Being a prying  sort of person she had followed Turkey Proudfoot around the house to see  what happened when he and the newcomer met.  "Don't be rude to this gentleman," said Henrietta Hen. "He hasn't  spoken since he arrived in the wagon an hour ago. We've about decided  that he is dumb. And it's a great pity if he is.
