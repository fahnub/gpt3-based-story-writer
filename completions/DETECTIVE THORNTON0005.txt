Celia was the first to observe the change, having nothing to do but  lie on the sofa and amuse herself by seeing others work or play. Ben was  bright enough at the readings, because then he forgot his troubles; but  when they were over and his various duties done, he went to his own room  or sought consolation with Lita, being sober and quiet, and quite unlike  the merry monkey all knew and liked so well.
