Twinkleheels watched sharply as the blacksmith captured a hind foot of  the white mare's and held it between his knees. Then he began to nail on  the shoe.  One thing puzzled Twinkleheels. Every time the blacksmith struck a blow  with his hammer he gave a funny grunt. Twinkleheels nudged Ebenezer with  his nose.  "Do you hear that?" he asked.
