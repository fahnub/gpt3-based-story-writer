suddenly there came a sound that made the Beaver family jump. It  came from the house--there was no doubt of that.  In fact it came right through the keyhole; and it was like nothing in  the world but a sneeze.  A number of people were all ready to jump into the water and swim away,  they were so startled.  And then a snicker followed the sneeze. And by that time Grandaddy  Beaver and his friends guessed who was inside the building. It was  Ferdinand Frog; and he had been watching his callers
