Back from the edge of the floe!      We tracked our seal to his secret place,      We heard him scratch below,    We made our mark, and we watched beside,      Out on the edge of the floe.      We raised our lance when he rose to breathe,      We drove it downward--so!    And we played him thus, and we killed him thus,      Out on the edge of the floe.
