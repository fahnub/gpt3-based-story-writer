But at last he came to the old poplar where his mother  lived. And soon, to his great joy, he reached the hole in the big  branch; and you may well believe that Fatty was glad to slip down into  the darkness where his mother, and his brother Blackie, and Fluffy and  Cutey his sisters, were all fast asleep.
