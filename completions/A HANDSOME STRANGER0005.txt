And to Mrs.  Ladybug's amazement, when she related the name of the stranger, and the  place he came from, Peppery Polly laughed in her face.  "Mr. P. Bug is not from Colorado," said Peppery Polly Bumblebee. "He has  never been off this farm."  Well, Mrs. Ladybug was staggered. She gasped. She clung to a leaf to  keep from failing.  "I don't believe that!" she cried, as soon as she could speak. "I'll  find Mr. Bug himself and learn the truth from him."
