When  passed  a  little  while,  he  told  again  Mr.  Elephant,  saying:  "Let  me    seek  a  green  twig  to  fan  the  mosquitoes  off  thee."    Mr.  Elephant  says:    "Go."    He  then  fetches  the  twig.  Then,  when  they  were  about  to  arrive,  the  girls  saw  them,  and  they  went    to  meet  them  with  shouting,  saying:  "Thou,  Mr.  Elephant,  art  the  horse    indeed  of  Mr.  Frog!"
