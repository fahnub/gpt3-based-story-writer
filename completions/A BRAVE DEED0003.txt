And nobody cared to go near  the place where he had sat, to see what had become of him.  At dawn Miss Kitty Cat crawled out of the woodpile to do a little early  hunting. And she claimed that at that hour Jack O'Lantern still sat on  the fence post. She saw the back of his head--so she said. And that was  enough for her. She did not look at him a second time.
