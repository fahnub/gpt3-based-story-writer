While he told them what the bays had said to him  they chewed their cuds and listened with a dreamy look in their great,  mild eyes.  Twinkleheels paused and waited for them to speak. But they said nothing.  Their jaws moved steadily as they chewed; but they said never a word.  "Can't you answer when you're spoken to?" Twinkleheels cried at last.
