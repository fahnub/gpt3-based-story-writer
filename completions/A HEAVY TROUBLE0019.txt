No one could grant that wish; but Miss Celia did comfort him, for  presently the sound of music floated out from the parlor,--music so  soft, so sweet, that involuntarily the boy stopped his crying to listen;  then quieter tears dropped slowly, seeming to soothe his pain as they  fell,
