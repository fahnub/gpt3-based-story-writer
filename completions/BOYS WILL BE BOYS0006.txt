"You haven't given up going  fishing--have you?"  "No!" Johnnie answered. "I'm going to harness Twinkleheels to the buggy.  And I'll cut a pole at the creek."  His father said nothing more. But he smiled a little to himself when  Johnnie wasn't looking his way.  "Boys will be boys," Farmer Green remarked after Johnnie had gone.  "Yes!" the hired man agreed. "And ponies will be ponies."  They may have been talking in riddles.  Anyhow, they seemed to understand each other.  THE END
