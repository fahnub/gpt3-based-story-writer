To his great relief, when he had gone down into Pleasant Valley Cuffy  saw all three ploughing in a field. They did not see him at all. And so  he felt very brave as he went on toward the farm buildings.  Farmer Green's pig-pen was in a little, low building next the cow-barn.  Cuffy had no trouble in finding it.
