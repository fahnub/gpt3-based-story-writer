'Where got you this silk?' asked the king.  'It was given me, royal majesty, by my mistress,' replied Catherine.  'That is not true,' said the king, 'and if you do not tell me the truthI will have your head cut off this instant.'  So Catherine told him the whole story, and how she had once been as richas he.
