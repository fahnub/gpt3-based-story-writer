He found Freddie in the swampy part of the meadow, near the place where  the cat-tails grew. And though Freddie was a bit sleepy, he became wide  awake the moment he heard Chirpy Cricket's voice.  "I've thought of a fine plan!" Chirpy Cricket cried. "I'm going to have  a torchlight procession and I want you and all your family to take part  in it."
