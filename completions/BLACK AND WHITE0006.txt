Snowball drank the milk he jerked his stubby tail up and down at  a great rate.  Old dog Spot, who was stretched upon the woodshed threshold, gazed at  Snowball with a lofty air.  "That lamb has a queer notion of the way a tail ought to be wagged," he  said deep down in his throat. "He ought to wag it from side to side. But  I suppose he's too young to know better."
