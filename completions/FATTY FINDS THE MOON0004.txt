Fatty cried, as they came in sight of the bright, round thing.  "There it is--just as I told you!" And they all set up a great shouting.  All but Mrs. Coon. She wasn't quite sure, even yet, that Fatty had  really found the moon. And she walked close to the shining thing and  peered at it. But not too close!
