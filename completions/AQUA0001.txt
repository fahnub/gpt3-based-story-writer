One of these was a  merry little fellow who danced on the silver backs of the fishes as they  plunged up and down in the waves, and, no matter how high he sprung,  always came down again plump into his mother's lap.  His mother, you know, was the Ocean, and very beautiful she looked that  summer day in her dark blue dress and white ruffles.
