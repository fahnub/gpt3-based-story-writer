Then first they discovered that the bolt they had shot, taking  him for one of the goblin creatures, had wounded him.  They carried him into the house and laid him down in the hall.  The  report spread that they had caught a robber, and the servants crowded  in to see the villain.  Amongst the rest came the nurse.
