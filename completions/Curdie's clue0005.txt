But as he scrambled to get up, his hand  fell upon his pickaxe, and before the horrid beasts could do him any  serious harm, he was laying about with it right and left in the dark.  The hideous cries which followed gave him the satisfaction of knowing  that he had punished some of them pretty smartly for their rudeness,  and by their scampering and their retreating howls, he perceived that  he had routed them.
