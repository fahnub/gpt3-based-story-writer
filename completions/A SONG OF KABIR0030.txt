"And those?" said Gray Brother, looking hungrily after the brown backs  of the charcoal-burners.  "Sing them home," said Mowgli, with a grin; "I do not wish them to be at  the village gates till it is dark. Can ye hold them?"  Gray Brother bared his white teeth in contempt.
