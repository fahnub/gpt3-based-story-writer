He made careful inquiries into the matter, and  thought it hardly probable that a quiet widow and a young girl would  think of attempting anything of the nature of a revolution.  He determined to go and see them, and to find out the truth for himself;  so one night, without saying anything to the Queen or his ministers, he  set out for the palace where the two ladies lived, attended only by a  small band of followers.
