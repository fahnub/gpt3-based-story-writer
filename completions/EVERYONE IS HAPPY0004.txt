At first he thought they were going to throw them at him;  and he was all ready to jump.  But he soon found that he was mistaken.  "Here! Here's your pay, Mr. Frog!" they began to cry. And to their  astonishment Mr. Frog began to laugh.  "I don't want any pay," he declared. "Will you all promise to wear your  new clothes if I make them free?"  "Yes! Yes!
