'Why yours, of course; I gave her to you,' said Mr. St. Clare.  'But not by law. There is no use my trying to make this child a Christian, unless I can be quite sure that she will not be sold as a slave again. If you are really willing I should have her, I want you to give me a paper saying she is mine.'
