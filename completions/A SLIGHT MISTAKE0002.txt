Mrs. Woodchuck said that nothing would please her more. So she ordered  Billy to scamper home.  "You'll have to wait till I put on my best poke," Aunt Polly said. "If  the Muley Cow has a new one I don't want to call on her in my second  best."  So Mrs. Woodchuck waited. And at last they set off together to find the  Muley Cow. They hadn't gone far before old Mr. Crow flapped down on a  hummock near them.  "If you're looking for the Muley Cow," he squawked, "you'll find her  down near the lane.
