Such hoots and howls, whistles and roars, never woke the echoes  of the lonely marsh before, or scared the portly frog who resided there  in calm seclusion.  He hardly expected any reply but the astonished "Caw!" of the crow, who  sat upon a fence watching him with gloomy interest; and when a cheerful  "Hullo, there!" sounded from the lane, he was so grateful that tears of  joy rolled down his fat cheeks.
