The angry Scoodler at once picked off his head and hurled it at the  Rainbow's Daughter; but the shaggy man was expecting that, and caught  the head very neatly, putting it in the sack, which he tied at the  mouth.  The body of the guard, not having the eyes of its head to guide  it, ran here and there in an aimless manner, and the shaggy man easily  dodged it and opened the door.
