'You've done it this time!' he said, then he fell heavily among the  flower-pots piled below. He heard them crash and rattle and crack, and  then his head struck against an iron pillar used for holding up the  next-door veranda. His eyes closed and he knew no more.  Now you will perhaps expect that at this moment Alice would have cried  'Murder!'
