There wasn't another of Mrs. Pig's children that could  squeeze through the opening.  Grunty Pig trotted the whole length of the lane. When he reached the  pasture he found himself face to face with the Muley Cow, who acted much  surprised to see him there.  "You'd better go back home at once," she advised him.
