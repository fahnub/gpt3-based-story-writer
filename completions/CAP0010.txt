"Who are you?" asked Trot, who was very curious and much surprised.  "I'm Cap'n Joe," was the reply. Why, dash my eyes, mate, you must be my brother!"  "Are YOU Bill Weedles?" asked the other. And then he added, "But no,  you can't be. Bill wasn't no mermaid. He were a human critter like  myself."
