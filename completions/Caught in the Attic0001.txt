And he couldn't help thinking how  kind Mr. Crow was, and how mistaken his mother had been to believe  that Mr. Crow liked to get folks into trouble.  "Come on!" said Mr. Crow, as Frisky paused beneath the tall tree. "I'm  going to fly over to that tree right next the farmhouse. You run along  the stone-wall and climb up beside me."
