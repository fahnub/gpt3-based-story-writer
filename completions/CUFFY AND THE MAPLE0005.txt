In the middle of the room was a big pan which gave off clouds of steam.  Cuffy wanted to see it. And with his mouth full of sugar he walked up to  the pan and looked into it. He saw a golden liquid, and Cuffy felt that  he simply _must_ taste that too. So he dipped both his front paws right  into the bubbling syrup.
