Bagheera. "The fever is out of my blood now. Let  them find ME there! Few would leave their houses after meeting me. It is  not the first time I have been in a cage; and I do not think they will  tie ME with cords."  "Be wise, then," said Mowgli, laughing; for he was beginning to feel as  reckless as the panther, who had glided into the hut.  "Pah!" Bagheera grunted.
