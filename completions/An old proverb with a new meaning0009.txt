"I think we'll be back before he wakes," said Mrs. Patterson, "and  anyhow he is very good and won't be any trouble. Don't let him go  outside, though. He has a cold now."  We went away, leaving Dan sitting on the door-sill reading his book, and  Jimmy P. snoozing blissfully on the sofa.
