'So she shall,' cried the king, who overheard these words; 'she shalldie my queen, for she is more beautiful than all the ladies of thecourt, and I will marry no one else.'  And so it fell out. The king sent back the bride he had promised to wedto her own country, and the same Catherine was queen at the marriagefeast instead, and lived happy and contented to the end of her life.
