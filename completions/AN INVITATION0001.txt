And being very  fond of water, Long Bill did not care to spend any of his valuable  time in Farmer Green's dooryard.  Of course, there was the duck pond not far away--and the river,  too. But the only water really close to Rusty's home was the  watering-trough. And that was entirely too small to please Long  Bill Wren. So no one ever saw him around the farm buildings.
