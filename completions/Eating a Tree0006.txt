Master Meadow Mouse chased his cousin no more, but hurried away to  Farmer Green's orchard, where he gnawed a ring all the way around one of  the young fruit trees, at the top of the snow. It was the first big meal  he had enjoyed for weeks. And he went home feeling that the winter was  not so hard as he had thought, after all.  But Farmer Green didn't agree with him.
