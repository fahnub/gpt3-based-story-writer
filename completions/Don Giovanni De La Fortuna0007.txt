And DonGiovanni left the inn and dwelt in the beautiful rooms, where his ragsand dirt looked sadly out of place. And every day these got worse and worse.  By-and-bye the fame of his riches reached the ears of the king, and, as he himself was always in need of money, he sent for Don Giovanni, as he wished to borrow a large sum.
