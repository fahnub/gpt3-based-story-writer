Dorothy willingly agreed, being the only other Princess in the Emerald  City; so she went to the great Throne-Room and sat in Ozma's seat,  placing Polly on one side of her and Button-Bright on the other.  The  Scarecrow stood at the left of the throne and the Tin Woodman at the  right, while the Wonderful Wizard and the shaggy man stood behind.  The Cowardly Lion and the Hungry Tiger came in, with bright new bows of  ribbon on their collars and tails.
