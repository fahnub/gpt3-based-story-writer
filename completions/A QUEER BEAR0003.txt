And he  enjoyed his holiday to the full--until he remembered suddenly that he  had been gone a long time--a much longer time than he had planned to  spend away from the farmyard.  "Oh, dear!" he whined. "Mother must be awake now; and she'll punish me  if I go back." The more he thought about returning, the less he liked  the idea.  "I won't go home at all!" he cried at last.
