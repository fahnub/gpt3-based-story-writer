Before Mr. Crow knew what had happened, a voice  said--right over his shoulder:  "You don't mind, I hope?"  It was Fatty Coon! And Mr. Crow certainly did mind--though he didn't dare  say so. In the first place, Mr. Crow was afraid of Fatty Coon. And in the  second place, Fatty was so big that he crowded Mr. Crow almost off the  fence.
