'Of course I shall!' returned Blockhead-Hans; and the brothers laughed  and rode on a good way.  'Slap! bang! here I am!' cried Blockhead-Hans; 'better and better--it is  really famous!'  'What have you found now?' asked the brothers.  'Oh,' said Blockhead-Hans, 'it is really too good! How pleased the  Princess will be!'
