Sam had seen Ben hop nimbly from one tuft of grass to another when he  went to gather cowslips for Betty, and the stout boy thought he could do  the same. Two or three heavy jumps landed him, not among the bulrushes,  as he had hoped, but in a pool of muddy water, where he sank up to his  middle with alarming rapidity.
