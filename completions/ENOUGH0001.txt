"I must try some other plan," thought Mrs. Ladybug. "And I'll have to  have help." So she sent Miss Moth over to the meadow, to find Freddie  Firefly and ask him if he wouldn't come to the orchard because Mrs.  Ladybug wanted to talk with him.  He came.
