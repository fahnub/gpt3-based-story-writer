Fatty was very fond of bugs--to eat, you know.  So he lost no time. The bright thing had scarcely settled on the water  when Fatty reached out and seized it. He put it into his mouth, when the  strangest thing happened. Fatty felt himself pulled right over into the  water.  He was surprised, for he never knew a bug or a fly to be so strong as  that.
