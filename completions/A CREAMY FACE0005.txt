He scrambled up a stepladder that leaned against  the wall and stood on the top of it while he pawed the air frantically,  as if he were trying to fly.  The noise brought Mrs. Green hurriedly out of the buttery. And she was  just in time to see Miss Kitty Cat peer out of the old stove-pipe hole,  with a _creamy_ look about her mouth.
