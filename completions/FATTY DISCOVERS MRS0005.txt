For he did not have to paw away much of the sand before he came  upon--what do you suppose? Eggs! Turtles' eggs! Twenty-seven round,  white eggs, which Mrs. Turtle had left there in the warm sand to hatch.  THAT was why she looked all around to make sure that no one saw her.  THAT was why she seemed so pleased.
