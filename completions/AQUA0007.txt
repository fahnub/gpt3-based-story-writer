Soon, together  they plunged into a mountain brook, which came foaming and dashing  along, leaping over rocks and rushing down the hillside, till in the  valley below they heard the strangest clattering noise.  On the bank stood a flour-mill, and at the door a man whose hat and  clothes were gray with dust.
