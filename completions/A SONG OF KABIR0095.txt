It was a dark night when Hathi and his three sons slipped down from the  Jungle, and broke off the poles of the machans with their trunks; they  fell as a snapped stalk of hemlock in bloom falls, and the men that  tumbled from them heard the deep gurgling of the elephants in their  ears.
