'That was neatly done!' said the Princess. 'I couldn't have done it; but  I will soon learn how to!'  Blockhead-Hans became King, got a wife and a crown, and sat on the  throne; and this we have still damp from the newspaper of the editor and  the reporters--and they are not to be believed for a moment.
