The decorations were  mostly birds and other fowl, such as peacocks, pheasants, turkeys,  prairie-chickens, ducks, and geese.  Over each doorway was carved a  head representing the fox who lived in that house, this effect being  quite pretty and unusual.  As our friends marched along, some of the foxes came out on the porches  and balconies to get a view of the strangers.
