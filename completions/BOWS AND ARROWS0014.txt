Matty lived alone with her father, but felt quite safe in the  log house, for he was never far away. One afternoon, as the farmers were  all busy in their fields, the bell rang suddenly,--a sign that there  was danger near,--and, dropping their rakes or axes, the men hurried  to their houses to save wives and babies, and such few treasures as they  could.
