"What are you doing here at this hour?" Mr. Crow demanded at last.  "I thought----" Dickie began.  "There you go again!" the old gentleman interrupted him testily. "I  didn't ask you what you _thought_. I asked you what you were _doing_."  "I'm not doing anything just now," Dickie Deer Mouse faltered.  "Yes, you are!"
