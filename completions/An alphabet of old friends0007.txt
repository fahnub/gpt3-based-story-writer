Taffy was a thief,  Taffy came to my house,   And stole a leg of beef.    I went to Taffy's house,   Taffy was not at home;  Taffy came to my house   And stole a marrow-bone.    I went to Taffy's house,   Taffy was in bed;  I took the marrow-bone,   And broke Taffy's head.
