And to Turkey Proudfoot he said, "Do you  mean to tell me that you don't like cranberries? Why, I've always heard  Turkey and cranberry sauce mentioned together."  "Ah!" said Turkey Proudfoot. "I've no doubt you've heard them spoken of  only too often. But that's no reason why I should be fond of cranberry  sauce. To tell the truth, all my life I've schemed to keep away from  it."
