And from that day himself he kept the gate    Wide open; and the poor from far and wide,    The weary, and wicked, and disconsolate,    Came there for succour and were not denied;    The sick were healed, the repentant sanctified;    And from their hearts rises more prayer and praise    Than ever the abbey knew in all its prayer-filled days.
