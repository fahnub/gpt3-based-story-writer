It was a  crow. It was the rooster's crow, way down at the farmyard.  "Why, it's almost dawn!" Turkey Proudfoot exclaimed. "I didn't know the  night was so nearly gone. It's no wonder I couldn't sleep. The dawn of  another day always makes one wide awake."  "It always makes one sleepy, you mean," Simon Screecher corrected him.  Now, Turkey Proudfoot always grew angry when anybody corrected him in  any way. And he flew into a rage.  "Go away! Go home!" he spluttered. "I don't enjoy your company."
