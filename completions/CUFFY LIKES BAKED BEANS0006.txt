Silkie all laughed so  hard at the sight of him that Cuffy began to whimper. And a big tear  rolled from each eye, ran down the bean-pot, and dropped off the bottom  of it.  And then, with just one tug Mr. Bear pulled the bean-pot off his son's  nose; and Cuffy was himself again.  He escaped a punishing, too, that time. And Mrs. Bear was very glad to  get such a nice iron pot. She had wanted one for a long time.
