With Johnnie Green tugging steadily, Twinkleheels changed from a run to  a canter, from a canter to a trot, from a trot to a walk; and finally  stood still.  Then Johnnie turned him around and rode slowly back to the barn. He  jumped down, unbuckled the girth, and drew off Twinkleheels' saddle.  "What's the matter?" his father asked him.
