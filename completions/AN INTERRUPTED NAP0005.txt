"You told me once," he reminded his mother, "that a Fox was a sly fellow  with a bushy tail and a long pointed nose. And this person in the tree  had----"  "Yes! Yes!" said his mother. "Now listen to what I say: A Fox is red.  And his tail has no rings at all. And Foxes don't climb trees."  "Yes, Mother!" was Nimble's meek answer.  He was glad to learn all that.
