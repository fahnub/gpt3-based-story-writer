"Maybe this will, then," said Mr. Chippy, quietly. And darting at Jasper  Jay, he knocked him off the stone wall before Jasper knew what was  happening.  Jasper Jay was furious. He scrambled quickly back upon the wall. But Mr.  Chippy had vanished. He had dived under the cover of the grapevine and  hid in a chink between the stones, where Jasper could not find him.
