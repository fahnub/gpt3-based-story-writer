Of course when old dog Spot chased--and  cornered--her, she was quite a different sort of creature. Then she  arched her back, puffed her tail out to twice its usual size, and spat  fiercely at Spot. He learned not to get within reach of her sharp claws,  when she behaved in that fashion. For old Spot had a tender nose. And no  one knew it better than Miss Kitty Cat.
