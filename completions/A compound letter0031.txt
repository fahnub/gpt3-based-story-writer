I did not keep a copy of my own letter, and I have forgotten everything  that was in it, except the first sentence, in which I told Peter I was  awful glad he was getting better.  Peter's delight on receiving our letters knew no bounds. He insisted  on answering them and his letter, painstakingly disinfected, was duly  delivered to us.
