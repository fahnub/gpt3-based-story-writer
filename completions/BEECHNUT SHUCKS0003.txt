When Frisky reached the farmyard he crept around a corner of the barn,  hoping to find a few kernels of corn. But Henrietta Hen had been there  before him and there wasn't one kernel left. He ran here and there about  the yard. And at last, when quite near the woodshed door, he sat up  suddenly, twitched his nose a few times, and said, "Ha! I smell  beechnuts!"  Now, that was not strange. Johnnie Green
