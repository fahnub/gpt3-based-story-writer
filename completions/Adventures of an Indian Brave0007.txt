The woman had been surprised before at the disappearance of her food,but she was more astonished still when, instead of the plank, shebeheld a baby.However, she was fond of children and had none of herown, so she made up her mind that she would keep it and take care ofit.The baby grew and throve as no baby in that country had ever done,and in four days he was a man, and as tall and strong as any brave ofthe tribe.
