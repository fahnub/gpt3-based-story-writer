We did bury it after tea, in the depths of the spruce grove; and then we  went into the orchard. It was so nice to have the Story Girl back again.  She had wreathed her hair with Canterbury Bells, and looked like the  incarnation of rhyme and story and dream.  "Canterbury Bells is a lovely name for a flower, isn't it?" she said.
