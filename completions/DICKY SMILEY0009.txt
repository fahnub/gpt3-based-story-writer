"Yes, indeed," answered the little girl, whose name was Lola. "I ran  behind the cart all the way to the pound. I cried after Bruno, and Bruno  whined for me, and poked his nose between the bars and tried to jump  out, but he couldn't. It's a pretty long way there, and the man is as  cross as two sticks."
