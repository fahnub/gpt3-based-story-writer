And then he  found a box half-full of something--scraps that tasted like apples, only  they were not round like apples, and they were quite dry, instead of  being juicy. But Fatty liked them; and he ate them all, down to the  smallest bit.  He was thirsty, then. So he went down to the brook, which ran close by  the camp.
