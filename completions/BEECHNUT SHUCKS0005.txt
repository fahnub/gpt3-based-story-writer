Squirrel's nose twitched  again, as he muttered to himself, "There's a very queer smell about  these beechnut shucks!"  He was sitting on the edge of the stone doorstep with a bit of beechnut  clutched in his paws. And when he looked up and saw somebody's nose  appear in the doorway he tumbled right over backward. The only sound he  made came from the beechnut shuck, which made a faint click as it fell  upon the stone. And Miss Kitty Cat's sharp ears caught it.
