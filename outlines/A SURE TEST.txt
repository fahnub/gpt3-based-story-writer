A young boy is being taught by his father how to be a gentleman. Part of being a gentleman is being able to control one's emotions and not laugh or smile when being tickled. The young boy is put to the test when his father tickles his knee. The boy does his best to control his laughter, but eventually succumbs to the tickling and laughs. His father is proud of him for trying and tells him that he will make a great gentleman one day.
