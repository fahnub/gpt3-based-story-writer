The story follows Daddy Longlegs as he tries to impress Betsy Butterfly. He is interrupted by Mrs. Ladybug who accuses Betsy of being a thief. Daddy Longlegs tries to explain that he saw Betsy on a butter-and-eggs plant, but Mrs. Ladybug is skeptical. Buster Bumblebee speaks up and backs Daddy Longlegs' claim, and Mrs. Ladybug is forced to apologize to Betsy.
