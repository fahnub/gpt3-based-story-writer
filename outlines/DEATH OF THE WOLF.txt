When Little Red Riding-Hood sets off to visit her grandmother, she is unaware that a wolf is following her. The wolf soon catches up to her and tricks her into telling him where she is going.

The wolf then goes to the grandmother's house, pretending to be Little Red Riding-Hood. The grandmother is fooled and let the wolf into the house. The wolf then eats the grandmother.

When Little Red Riding-Hood arrives at the house, she is also fooled and let the wolf in. The wolf then prepares to eat her as well.

At the last minute, the woodman Hugh arrives and saves both Little Red Riding-Hood and her grandmother. He kills the wolf with his axe.

Everyone is safe and they all live happily ever after.
