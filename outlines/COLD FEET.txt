Jimmy Rabbit tells Jasper Jay to stand in a puddle of water by a cedar tree in order to cure him of his fear of Reddy Woodpecker. Jasper agrees, but finds the experience quite unpleasant. Jimmy tells him to remain still and quiet, and Jasper eventually complies. Jimmy then goes to find Reddy Woodpecker and tells him that Jasper is trying to cure his fear of Reddy by standing in a puddle of water. Reddy is amused and amused by Jasper's predicament.
