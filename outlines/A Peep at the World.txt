Master Meadow Mouse is exploring the world outside of his nest for the first time and is amazed by all the new sights and sounds. His mother warns him of the dangers of venturing too far from home, but he doesn't listen and eventually meets a kitten from the farmhouse. The kitten is friendly at first, but then tries to catch Master Meadow Mouse. He barely escapes and runs all the way home to his mother. She scolds him and he promises to never wander too far from home again.
