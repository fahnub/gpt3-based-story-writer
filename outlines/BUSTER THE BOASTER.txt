Buster Bumblebee is a young bee who is afraid of the Robber Fly, a fearsome creature that is said to lurk in the flower garden and clover field. When Buster's father is caught by the Robber Fly, the young bee is determined to find and confront the villain. After a long search, Buster finally finds the Robber Fly's lair and bravely faces him down. The Robber Fly is impressed by Buster's courage and decides to leave the area for good.
