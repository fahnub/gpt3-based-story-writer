Mrs. Ladybug's friends miss her after she goes into the farmhouse to stay for the winter. They spread rumors that she is back, and sure enough, she is. However, she is acting strange and will not answer any questions about her winter stay. Her friends try to get her to tell them what happened, but she refuses. Finally, Buster Bumblebee makes a blundering comment and Mrs. Ladybug finally tells them that she and Mrs. Green did not get along well together.
