Solomon Owl sets out to tell Mr. Crow that he is mistaken about Grumpy Weasel being able to fill knot holes at the sawmill. He explains that Grumpy Weasel is too active to do such a thing. Mr. Crow gets angry and tells Solomon Owl that he is the one who is mistaken. He says that Grumpy Weasel is too short-tempered to be welcome anywhere.
