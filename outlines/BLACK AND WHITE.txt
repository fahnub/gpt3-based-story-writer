Johnnie Green is excited to learn that he may be able to get a lamb for a pet. He runs to the barnyard to try to catch a black lamb, but the lamb is too quick for him. Johnnie's father helps him catch the lamb, but Johnnie soon discovers that the lamb is too wild to be a pet. The lamb runs away and Johnnie is disappointed.

The next day, Johnnie's father comes home with a white lamb. Johnnie is excited to have a pet lamb of his own and names him Snowball. Snowball is a very active lamb and Johnnie has to be careful that he doesn't switch his tail off.
