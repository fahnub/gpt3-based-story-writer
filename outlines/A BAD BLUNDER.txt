Grandaddy Beaver and his friends go to visit Ferdinand Frog at the pond, but he is nowhere to be found. They discover a small building nearby and soon realize that it is where Ferdinand Frog lives. They hear a noise coming from the house and soon realize that it is Ferdinand Frog, who has been watching and listening to them the whole time. Ferdinand Frog is a tailor and offers to make clothes for them. They form a line to go into the house and Ferdinand Frog measures them. They are supposed to pass out the back way, but Ferdinand Frog makes a blunder and they all crowd out the front door.
