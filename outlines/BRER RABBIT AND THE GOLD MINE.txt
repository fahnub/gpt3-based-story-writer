Brer Rabbit and the other animals are in search of a gold mine that is rumoured to be in the area. Brer Rabbit is in a group with Brer Wolf, and he knows that he has to keep his eyes open. The animals all agree to search for the gold mine together, and they start digging in different places. Brer Rabbit makes a lot of motion like he is digging, andBre Wolf calls him over to show him something. Brer Rabbit pretends to be interested and says he will take the leftovers. Brer Wolf makes a dash for Brer Rabbit, but Brer Rabbit is gone. Brer Wolf calls out after him, but Brer Rabbit just tells him that he is going home to get a bag to carry the gold in and then he will be back.
