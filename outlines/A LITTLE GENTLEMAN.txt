Dickie Deer Mouse is a well-liked and well-mannered mouse who is respected by all of the four-legged creatures in Pleasant Valley. He is always tastefully dressed and is known for being clean and tidy. Dickie is also very fast and agile, able to scamper through the woods quickly and effortlessly.

One of Dickie's best qualities is his resourcefulness. He is known for gathering food and storing it away in a safe place, so that he can survive the winter months when food is scarce. This trait makes Dickie a popular figure among his animal friends, who admire his ability to remain cheerful and upbeat even in the dead of winter.
