Brownie Beaver is building a new house and is warned by Tired Tim that a cyclone is coming that will blow away all the houses in the village. Brownie Beaver goes to ask Grandaddy Beaver about the cyclone and he confirms that it is coming. Brownie Beaver tries to warn his neighbors but they don't believe him. Brownie Beaver is worried about his new house being blown away and he tries to think of a way to protect it.
