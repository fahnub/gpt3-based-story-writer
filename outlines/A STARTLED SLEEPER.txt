Dickie Deer Mouse is looking for a new home, and has heard that Mr. Crow is moving. He goes to Mr. Crow's house to investigate, and finds the old gentleman asleep. When Dickie accidentally wakes him, Mr. Crow is startled and angry. He demands to know what Dickie is doing there, and Dickie explains that he thought Mr. Crow was moving. Mr. Crow tells him to leave, and Dickie does so. It is clear that Dickie will have to find a new home elsewhere.
