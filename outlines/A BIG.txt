A big, fat potato dreams that he is the king of the garden and has to be obeyed by all the plants. He makes the radishes dance, the cucumbers sing, the cabbages grow, and the pumpkins blue. He strutted and sputtered and thought it was grand to be king and commander o'er all the wide land. But at last he woke up with an awful surprise and found a blind mole kicking sand in his eyes.
