Rusty Wren brings home a ladybug to his wife, Mrs. Wren, who is pleased with the insect. However, Rusty soon realizes that the beetle tastes terrible and drops it on the ground. Mrs. Wren is upset with Rusty for bringing home the beetle and scolds him. Meanwhile, the beetle is nowhere to be found. Mrs. Wren talks to her friends about the incident later and they all agree that Rusty must have made a mistake.
