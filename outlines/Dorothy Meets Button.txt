Dorothy, the shaggy man, and Toto meet a boy named Button-Bright who is lost and doesn't know anything. They decide to take him with them on their journey. Button-Bright is wearing sailor clothes but has never been to sea. He tells them he just started digging a hole when they came up to him. They ask him where he lives and he says he doesn't know. They ask him where he came from and he says he doesn't know. They ask him what he was going to do and he says he doesn't know. They ask him his name and he says it's Button-Bright. Dorothy gets frustrated with him and calls him stupid. The shaggy man says they should just take him with them. Toto starts digging in the hole and gets dirt all over Button-Bright. Dorothy scolds Toto and they start on their way.
