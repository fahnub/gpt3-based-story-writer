Mrs. Ladybug is jealous of Betsy Butterfly's beauty and begins to spy on her in the hope of finding some fault. Betsy is always polite to Mrs. Ladybug, even when she is met multiple times a day. Mrs. Ladybug is touchy about her family and tells Betsy not to ask about them. Betsy then asks if Mrs. Ladybug likes butter and Mrs. Ladybug says she doesn't know because she's never tried it. Betsy tells her that she can tell by looking into a buttercup and Mrs. Ladybug does. Mrs. Ladybug's face turns yellow, which means she likes butter. Mrs. Ladybug is pleased and decides to go to the farmhouse to ask for some.
