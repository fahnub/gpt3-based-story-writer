Rusty Wren's cousin, Long Bill, is having a party tomorrow at his home near Black Creek and has sent old Mr. Crow to deliver the invitation. When Jolly Robin arrives with the message, Rusty is excited to attend but his wife is not pleased as it is a men's party. Rusty promises to help his wife with her house-cleaning tomorrow but is clearly eager to attend the party. His wife warns him that he will be busy at home. The next day, Rusty goes to the party while his wife stays home to clean.
