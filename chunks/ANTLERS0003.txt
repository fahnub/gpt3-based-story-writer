He had expected to have a pleasant time showing his new antlers to his  old friends. When he met Dodger the Deer, Nimble called to him: "See  what I've got! Antlers! Two points!"  "Ho!" said Dodger. "So have I got antlers. And they have two points,  too."  Nimble had been so interested in his own horns that he hadn't looked at  Dodger's.
