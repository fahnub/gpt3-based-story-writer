A HERMIT'S ADVICE (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    THERE was another, besides Timothy Turtle, who was not pleased when  Bobby Bobolink moved to Cedar Swamp at haying time. But this was a  very different sort of person. It was Jolly Robin's cousin, Mr. Hermit  Thrush. Everybody called him "the Hermit" for short, because he was a  quiet gentleman, who did not like to attract attention, but preferred  to spend his time in a thicket on the edge of the swamp.
