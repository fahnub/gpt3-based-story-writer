These grew, as he went on,  to a scuffling and growling and squeaking; and the noise increased,  until, turning a second sharp corner, he found himself in the midst of  it, and the same moment tumbled over a wallowing mass, which he knew  must be a knot of the cobs' creatures.  Before he could recover his  feet, he had caught some great scratches on his face and several severe  bites on his legs and arms.
