Ben thanked his stars, as he had already done more than once, that he  knew how to take care of a horse; for he delayed by the watering-place  long enough to wash out Lita's mouth with a handful of wet grass, to let  her have one swallow to clear her dusty throat, and then went slowly  back over the breezy hills, patting and praising the good creature for  her intelligence and speed.
