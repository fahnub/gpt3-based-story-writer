Simon Screecher started homewards at once.  "Farmyard manners!" he muttered. "I declare, I wish Cousin Solomon  hadn't eaten those two mice and those three frogs and those four spiders  and those five grasshoppers to-night. When he's well fed he's always  good-natured. If he had been hungry he'd have been in a terrible temper.  And he'd have fought this Turkey bird until there was nothing left of  him but his tail feathers."
