FATTY COON AT HOME (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    Fatty Coon was so fat and round that he looked like a ball of fur, with  a plumelike tail for a handle. But if you looked at him closely you  would have seen a pair of very bright eyes watching you.  Fatty loved to eat. Yes--he loved eating better than anything else in  the world.
