She said that before starting she expected to wait for  the wagon, that she hoped to stay at the fair as long as it lasted  (because she didn't want to miss anything!) and that she intended to come  home when the wagon brought her. Furthermore, she planned to wear her  best apron, anyhow, because there was sure to be fair weather at a fair!  How could it be otherwise?  Old Ebenezer, the horse, told her to be sure to see the races.
