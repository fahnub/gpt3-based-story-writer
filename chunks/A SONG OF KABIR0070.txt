minute  Bagheera raised his head and yawned--elaborately, carefully, and  ostentatiously--as he would yawn when he wished to insult an equal.  The fringed lips drew back and up; the red tongue curled; the lower jaw  dropped and dropped till you could see half-way down the hot gullet; and  the gigantic dog-teeth stood clear to the pit of the gums till they rang  together, upper and under, with the snick of steel-faced wards shooting  home round the edges of a safe.
