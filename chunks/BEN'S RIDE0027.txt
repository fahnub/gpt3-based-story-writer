Oh, what shall we do?"  As if she knew exactly what was wanted of her, Lita went back the way  she had come, as Ben could see by the fresh, irregular tracks that cut  up the road where she had galloped for help. For a mile or more they  went, then she paused at a pair of bars, which were let down to allow  the carts to pass into the wide hay-fields beyond.
