DICKIE'S SUMMER HOME (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    Dickie Deer Mouse was busier than ever. When he wasn't looking for  food--and eating it when he had found it--he gathered cat-tail down in  Cedar Swamp.  If there was one thing that he liked in a house it was a soft bed. And  he knew that if the weather happened to be chilly now and then, he could  snuggle into the cat-tail down and sleep as comfortably as he pleased.
