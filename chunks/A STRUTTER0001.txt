A STRUTTER(Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    All the hen turkeys thought Turkey Proudfoot a wonderful creature. They  said he had the most beautiful tail on the farm. When he spread it and  strutted about Farmer Green's place the hen turkeys were sure to nudge  one another and say, "Ahem! Isn't he elegant?"
