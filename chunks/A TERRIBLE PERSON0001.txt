A TERRIBLE PERSON (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    THE rats and the mice thought that Miss Kitty Cat was a terrible person.  She was altogether too fond of hunting them. They agreed, however, that  in one way it was pleasant to have her about the farmhouse. When she  washed her face, while sitting on the doorsteps, they knew--so they  said!--that it was going to rain.
