Mr. Frog exclaimed, buttoning the long coat which he had donned before  going out of doors. "I wish they'd hurry up!" he added mysteriously. He  kept a close watch upon his shop door. It was evident that he expected  callers.  Not long afterward a crowd began to gather in front of Mr. Frog's door.  "Back Soon" said the sign upon it.
