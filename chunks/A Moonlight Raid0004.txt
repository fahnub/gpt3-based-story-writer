Again he roused Master Meadow Mouse from his slumbers.  "He's at it again!" Master Meadow Mouse complained. "I wish he'd go to  some other shock."  The third time that Fatty Coon wrenched an ear of corn from the shock  where Master Meadow Mouse lived he paused and cocked an ear towards the  top of the shock.
