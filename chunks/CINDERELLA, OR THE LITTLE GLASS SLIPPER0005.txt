"For my part," said the elder, "I will wear my red velvet suit with French trimmings."  "And I," said the younger, "shall wear my usual skirt; but then, to make amends for that I will put on my gold-flowered mantle, and my diamond stomacher, which is far from being the most ordinary one in the world."
