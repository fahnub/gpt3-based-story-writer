A LITTLE SURPRISE (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    Little by little the Muley Cow learned not to be disturbed by Johnnie  Green's clothesline lasso, when he swung it in wide circles about his  head and then flung it at hers. She found that the rope did her no harm.  Indeed, the more Johnnie practiced the more expert he became.
