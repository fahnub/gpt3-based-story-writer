Cuffy had a happy thought. He would go out and shove his paws into  a snowbank. That would surely cool them. So out of the sugar-house he  dashed and across the clearing he ran, screaming _"Ough! ough! ough!"_  at the top of his voice, for the hot syrup made his paws smart terribly.  In his haste Cuffy did not notice that he was headed in the direction in  which the man had disappeared.
