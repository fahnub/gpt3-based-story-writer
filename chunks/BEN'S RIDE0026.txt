Ben was up like a flash,  tingling all over with a sense of power as he felt the bare back between  his knees, and caught the roll of Lita's eye as she looked round with an  air of satisfaction.  "Hi, there! Mrs. Moss! Something has happened to Miss Celia, and I'm  going to find her. Thorny is asleep; tell him easy, and I'll come back  as soon as I can!"
