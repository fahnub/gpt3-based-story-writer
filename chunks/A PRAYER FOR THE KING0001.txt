A PRAYER FOR THE KING'S MAJESTY (Poetry:- Ages 3 to 5)      22nd January, 1901.    THE Queen is dead. God save the King,    In this his hour of grief,    When sorrow gathers memories in a sheaf    To lay them on his shoulders as he stands    Inheriting her glories and her lands--    First gain of his at which his Mother's voice    Has not been first to bless and to rejoice--    A man, set lonely between gain and loss.
