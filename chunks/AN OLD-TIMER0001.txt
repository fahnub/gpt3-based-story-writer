AN OLD-TIMER (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    It was pleasant for Timothy Turtle that he lived in Black Creek, for he  was very fond of fishing. If he had happened to make his home among the  rocks on the top of Blue Mountain he would have had to travel a long way  to find even a trout stream. But in Black Creek there were fish right in  his dooryard, one may say.  It was lucky for him, too, that he liked fish to eat.
