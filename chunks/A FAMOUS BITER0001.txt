A FAMOUS BITER (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    That black rascal, Mr. Crow, was not the oldest dweller in Pleasant  Valley. There was another elderly gentleman who had spent more  summers--and a great many more winters--under the shadow of Blue  Mountain than he.  All the wild folk knew this person by the name of Timothy Turtle. And if  they didn't see him so often as Mr. Crow it was because he spent much of  his time on the muddy bottom of Black Creek.
