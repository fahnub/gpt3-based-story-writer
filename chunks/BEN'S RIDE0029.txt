"May be she wasn't hurt, and has gone to that house to wait," thought  the boy, pausing for a last survey of the great, sunny field, which had  no place of shelter in it but one rock on the other side of the little  stream. As his eye wandered over it, something dark seemed to blow out  from behind it, as if the wind played in the folds of a shirt, or a  human limb moved.
