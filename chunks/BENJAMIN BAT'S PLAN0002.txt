Such thoughts, however, never entered Kiddie Katydid's head. He went  cheerfully about his business--which was _eating_, principally--and  jumped or flew as the mood seized him. Indeed, if it hadn't been for  that queer fellow, Benjamin Bat, probably Kiddie never would have  realized just what he could--or couldn't--do.
