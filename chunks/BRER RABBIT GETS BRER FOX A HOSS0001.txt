BRER RABBIT GETS BRER FOX A HOSS (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)     [Illustration]    Not many er de creeturs wuz fon' er water,   Onless it mought 'a' been Brer Coon's daughter;   Brer B'ar, Brer Fox, an' ol' Brer Rabbit,   Dey vow'd dey can't never git in de habit   Er wadin' de creek, er swimmin' de river--   When it come ter dat, dey'd run ter kivver!
