A SAFE PERCH (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    As Turkey Proudfoot faced the six geese in the farmyard he began to feel  that he had made a great mistake in speaking to them. Their hisses were  far from agreeable. They were even threatening.  "This will never do," Turkey Proudfoot muttered to himself. "No doubt I  could whip all six of them; but they'd be likely to pull some of my tail  feathers out. And I don't want my tail spoiled." For a moment or two he  didn't know what to do.
