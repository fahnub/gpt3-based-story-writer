After this had been received by  Dorothy with proper thanks and placed on the table with the other  presents, the visitors from Hiland and Loland were escorted to their  rooms by the High Chamberlain.  They had no sooner departed than the band before the palace began to  play again, announcing more arrivals, and as these were doubtless from  foreign parts the High Chamberlain hurried back to receive them in his  most official manner.
