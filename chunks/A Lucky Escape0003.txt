So he hugged the board tight. But the closer he came to Mr. Heron the  bigger and fiercer that gentleman looked.  Suddenly Master Meadow Mouse's courage oozed out through his toes. He  couldn't stay on his raft another second. Springing to his feet, he  scurried to the edge of the board and slipped off it into the water.  At his first move Mr. Heron moved too.
