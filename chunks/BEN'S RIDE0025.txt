"Lita, where's Miss Celia?" he asked, looking straight into the  intelligent eyes, which were troubled but not wild.  Lita threw up her head, and neighed loud and clear, as if she called her  mistress; and, turning, would have gone again if Ben had not caught the  reins and held her.
