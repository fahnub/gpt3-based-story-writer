There was another interesting feature of the scene which would have  puzzled any but those well acquainted with the manners and customs of  dolls. A fourteenth rag baby, with a china head, hung by her neck from  the rusty knocker in the middle of the door. A sprig of white and one of  purple lilac nodded over her, a dress of yellow calico, richly trimmed  with red-flannel
