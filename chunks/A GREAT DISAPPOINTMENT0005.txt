And in another twinkling it was outside again--for Mr. Crow did not like  the taste at all.  "That's a bad one!" he remarked. And then he tried another kernel--and  another--and another. But they were all like the first one.  Thereupon, Mr. Crow paused and looked at the corn. And he saw at once  that there was something wrong. The kernels were gray, instead of a  golden yellow.
