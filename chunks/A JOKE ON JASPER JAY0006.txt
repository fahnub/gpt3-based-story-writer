Since he dared not attack Mr. Crow, especially when his ten relations  were with him, there was nothing Jasper could do except give a loud,  helpless scream of rage and hurry away toward the woods.  "See those crows chasing that blue jay!" Farmer Green said to Johnnie,  as they walked toward home. "Probably he's played some trick on them."  But for once it was not Jasper who was guilty.
