Daylight at Last(Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)      Cap'n Bill rubbed his eyes, lit a match and consulted his watch.  "Nine o'clock.  Yes, I guess it's another day, sure enough. Shall we go  on?" he asked.  "Of course," replied the Ork. "Unless this tunnel is different from  everything else in the world, and has no end, we'll find a way out of  it sooner or later."  The sailor gently wakened Trot.
