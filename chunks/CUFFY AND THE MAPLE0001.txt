CUFFY AND THE MAPLE-SUGAR (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Another day had come and all the morning long Cuffy Bear and his sister  Silkie played and played as hard as they could. They played that they  were making maple-sugar. And they pretended to hang buckets on all the  trees near Mr. Bear's house. There were no maple trees about Cuffy's  home--only pine and hemlock and spruce--but if you are just _pretending_  to make maple-sugar any sort of tree will do.
