Prince, the King's only son appeared. He was dressed in white velvet and gold, and had a long feather in his cap. In the pleasantest way, he took Elidyr's hand and said:  "Glad to see you. Come and let us play together."  That was just what Elidyr liked to hear. The King smiled and said to his visitor, "You will attend my son?"
