Esben was soon back at the palace with the boar, and his brothersscarcely knew which leg to stand on, so rejoiced were they that theywere safe again. Not one of them, however, ever thought of thankingEsben for what he had done for them.  The king was still more rejoiced over the boar than he had been over thedove, and did not know what to give the brothers for it.
