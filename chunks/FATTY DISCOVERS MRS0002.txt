Fatty Coon  found himself on the bank of the creek which flowed slowly into Swift  River. Fatty had been looking for frogs, but he had had no luck at all.  To tell the truth, Fatty was a little too young to catch frogs easily,  even when he found one; and he was a good deal too fat, for he was so  plump that he was not very spry.
