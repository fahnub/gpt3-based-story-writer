And the result was that Tired Tim Beaver returned to the  village in the pond in a terrible state of mind. Since he told everyone  else what he had learned about Ferdinand Frog and his clothes, it was  only a short time before the whole Beaver family was so stirred up that  they couldn't do a stroke of work.
