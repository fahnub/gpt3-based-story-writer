In his greedy haste Fatty had merely bitten through  the cord that fastened the ham to the pole. And of course it had at once  fallen, carrying Fatty with it!  But what do you suppose? Afterward, when Fatty had grown up, and had  children of his own, he often told them about the time he had escaped  from the trap in Farmer Green's smokehouse.
