A QUEER OLD PERSON (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    THERE was a queer old person that lived in Farmer Green's garden. Nobody  knew exactly how long he had made his home there because his neighbors  seldom saw him. He might have been in the garden a whole summer before  anybody set eyes on him.  Those that were acquainted with him called him Grandfather Mole. And the  reason why his friends didn't meet him oftener was because he spent  most of his time underground. Grandfather Mole's house was in a mound
