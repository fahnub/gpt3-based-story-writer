Even on bright summer days this wood was cool and dark, and, as you  walked about on the soft brown carpet, you could hear the wonderful song  the pine needles made as they rubbed against each other; and perhaps far  away in the top of some tall tree you could hear the wood-thrush sing  out gladly.
