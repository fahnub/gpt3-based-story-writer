"Papa always said I was bright as a button; so mama always called me  Button-Bright," he said.  "What is your papa's name?"  "Just Papa."  "What else?"  "Don't know."  "Never mind," said the shaggy man, smiling.  "We'll call the boy  Button-Bright, as his mama does.  That name is as good as any, and  better than some."
