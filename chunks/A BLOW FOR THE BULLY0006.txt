I want you to laugh _loud_.  "You're going to be happy, if I have to break every bone in your body,"  Jasper added.  Naturally, that threat did not help little Mr. Chippy to laugh. Instead,  he looked quite worried. He knew that Jasper Jay was a bully. And there  was no telling what he might do to anyone so small as Mr. Chippy was. So  he tried his best to please Jasper. But he was so upset that he could  manage only a feeble "_Chip, chip, chip, chip!_"  "That'll never do," Jasper told him.
