A Moonlight Raid (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    THE Meadow Mouse party, in the cornfield, vanished as if by magic. Not  one of the merrymakers lingered an instant after hearing that Fatty Coon  was entering the field. And since Master Meadow Mouse happened to be  near the shock where he lived, he ran up it in a twinkling and crept  inside it, to curl up in his nest and try to catch forty winks.
