There was a lively scattering of those who found themselves in Timothy  Turtle's path. And for a time it looked as if the singing party had  broken up in disorder.  But after a while everybody came back again--that is, everybody but  Timothy Turtle. He hurried away and spent most of the whole night buried  in the mud at the bottom of Black Creek.
