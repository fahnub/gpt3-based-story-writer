CHLOE (Poetry:- Ages 3 to 5)      NIGHT wind sighing through the poplar leaves,    Trembling of the aspen, shivering of the willow,    Every leafy voice of all the night-time grieves,    Mourning, weeping over Chloe's pillow.      Chloe, fresher than the breeze of dawn,    Fairer than the larches in their young spring glory,    Brighter than the glow-worms on the dewy lawn,    Hear the dirge the green trees sing to end your story:--      "Chloe lived and Chloe loved: she brought new gladness,
