CAUGHT IN THE RAIN (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Left alone in the woods with Mr. Crow's umbrella, Jasper Jay had a fine  time. First he looked at the umbrella very closely, from the handle to  the slender tip. Then he placed it under his wing and strutted back and  forth upon the ground, just as he had seen Mr. Crow parade before his  friends. And Jasper wished that someone would come along and see him.
