A disobedient brother (Fiction Ages 5 to 10)  Dan was his own man again in the morning, though rather pale and weak;  he wanted to get up, but Cecily ordered him to stay in bed. Fortunately  Felicity forgot to repeat the command, so Dan did stay in bed. Cecily  carried his meals to him, and read a Henty book to him all her spare  time.
