"Who enters here can never return to Fairyland," she said, and her voice was sweeter than the songs of birds.  Fairy Tenderheart pushed open the gate and stepped within the Garden. "Who enters here finds joy," said the Oldest Fairy of All, and a crown of happiness sat on her hair.  "You come to work?" she asked.  "I come to learn what I may do to help the suffering earth," said Fairy Tenderheart.
