On this elegant couch lay Prince Rainbow, looking more beautiful than  ever, and sunk in profound slumber, in which he had been held ever since  his disappearance.  Fairy-than-a-Fairy, who now saw him for the first time in his real  shape, hardly dared to gaze at him, fearing lest his appearance might  not be in keeping with the voice and language which had won her heart.
