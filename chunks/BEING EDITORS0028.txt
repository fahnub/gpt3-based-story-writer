They had rifled the cellars of the hut, and the rich wines flowed like  water in the golden goblets of Mr Ridgway.  But Annie had made friends with one of the gang, a noble, good-hearted  man who had joined Sam Redfern by mistake, and she had told him to go  and get the police as quickly as possible.  'Ha! ha!' cried Redfern, 'now I am enjoying myself!' He little knew that  his doom was near upon him.
