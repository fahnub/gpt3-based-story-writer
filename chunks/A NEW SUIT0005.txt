Sandy  Chipmunk had a queer feeling then that he had met the fellow before.  That mouth had plenty of white, needle-like teeth. It had a cruel look,  too.  Then the stranger jumped straight toward Sandy Chipmunk. And in that  instant Sandy knew who he was. No one could leap like that except Grumpy  Weasel!  Sandy turned and ran madly for shelter.
