He sez ter Brer Coon, "Run here an' see de Moon!   A-floatin' widout a fin!"    [Illustration]    He look ag'in--"She sho fell in,   An' we got ter git her out;   Ef she stays in de pon', it's 'good-bye, John!'   An' uv dat dey ain't no doubt;  "We got ter have light when we play at night,   Fer ter see how ter git about;   We'll drag wid de seine--ef we don't drag in vain,   We'll have good reason ter shout!"
