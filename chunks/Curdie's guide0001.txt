Curdie's Guide (Fiction:- Ages 5 to 10)  Just as the consolation of this resolve dawned upon his mind and he was  turning away for the cellar to follow the goblins into their hole,  something touched his hand.  It was the slightest touch, and when he  looked he could see nothing.  Feeling and peering about in the grey of  the dawn, his fingers came upon a tight thread.  He looked again, and  narrowly, but still could see nothing.  It flashed upon him that this  must be the princess's thread.
