A Lucky Escape (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    NEARER and nearer the board, with Master Meadow Mouse upon it, drifted  around the bend of the creek toward Mr. Great Blue Heron. And at last  Mr. Heron noticed it. And he noticed its passenger, too.  "Ahem!" he said softly to himself. Except for swallowing once or twice,  he never made a move, but stood there in the water and waited.
