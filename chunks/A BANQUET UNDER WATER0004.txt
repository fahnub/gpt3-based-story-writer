There were no stairs here, because there was no  need of them, and the little girl soon found herself in an upper  room that was very beautiful indeed.  All the walls were covered with iridescent shells, polished till  they resembled mother-of-pearl, and upon the glass ceiling were  clusters of the brilliant electric jellyfish, rendering the room  bright and cheerful with their radiance.
