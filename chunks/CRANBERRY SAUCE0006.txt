He knew that it was useless to rush at  Mr. Crow. The old gentleman would only rise into the air and sail away  with a loud haw-haw.  Now, Mr. Crow was a famous tease. He dearly loved to annoy others. And  he gave Turkey Proudfoot a sly glance.  "Ouch!" he exclaimed. "I have a twinge of rheumatism."
