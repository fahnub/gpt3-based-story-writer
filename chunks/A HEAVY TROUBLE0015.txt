Presently the sobbing ceased, and Ben whispered, without  looking up,--  "Tell me all about it; I'll be good."  Then, as kindly as she could, Miss Celia read the brief letter which  told the hard news bluntly; for Mr. Smithers was obliged to confess that  he had known the truth months before, and never told the boy, lest he  should be unfitted for the work
