BEN'S RIDE (Fiction:- Ages 5 to 10)  Great was the mourning for Sancho, because his talents and virtues made  him universally admired and beloved. Miss Celia advertised, Thorny  offered rewards, and even surly Pat kept a sharp look-out for poodle  dogs when he went to market; but no Sancho or any trace of him appeared.  Ben was inconsolable, and sternly said it served Bab right when the  dogwood poison affected both face and hands. Poor Bab thought so, too,  and dared ask no sympathy from him,
