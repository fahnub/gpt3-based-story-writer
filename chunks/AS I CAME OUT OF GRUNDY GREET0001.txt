AS I CAME OUT OF GRUNDY GREET (Poetry:- Ages 0 to 3)    As I came out of Grundy Greet    Four cats were marching down the street      One was long and gray and thin    With lots of whiskers on his chin,      And one was round and sleek and fat    (He must have been a butcher's cat).      One was dapper, slight, and frail,    With bells and tassels on his tail,      And one had starey yellow eyes    Almost as big as pumpkin pies.
