Phoenix spokethrough the gloom.  'Peace!' it said; 'there are no bones here except the small but usefulsets that you have inside you. And you did not invite me to come outwith you to hear you talk about bones, but to see you do some good andkind action.'  'We can't do it here,' said Robert, sulkily.  'No,' rejoined the bird. 'The only thing we can do here, it seems, is totry to frighten our little sisters.'  'He didn't, really, and I'm not so VERY little,' said Jane, ratherungratefully.  Robert
