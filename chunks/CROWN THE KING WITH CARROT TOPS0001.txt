CROWN THE KING WITH CARROT TOPS (Poetry:- Ages 0 to 3)      Crown the king with carrot tops,    Dress him in sateen,    Give him lots of licorice drops,    With suckers in between.      For he's a king with lots of power    And awful, awful fierce,    He kills a pirate every hour    And washes in his tears.      He rides a charger ten feet high,    A dashing, dappled gray;    Has ginger pop and lemon pie    For breakfast every day.      So get a royal canopy,    The finest ever seen,    And
