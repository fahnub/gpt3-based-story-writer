Close  beside the door by which the princess left the garden for the mountain  stood a great rock, and by climbing it Curdie got over the wall.  He  tied his clue to a stone just inside the channel of the stream, and  took his pickaxe with him.  He had not gone far before he encountered a  horrid creature coming towards the mouth.
