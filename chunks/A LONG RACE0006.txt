As he spoke of feeling hungry he cast a longing glance at Jimmy Rabbit,  who was just dodging out of sight behind a distant tree.  "Wait here a bit, anyhow!" Mr. Crow urged him. "Since you're sure to  win--as you say--there can be no hurry." And Peter Mink too begged his  cousin Grumpy to stop just a minute. And he laughed, "Ha, ha!" whenever  he looked at Mr. Crow.
