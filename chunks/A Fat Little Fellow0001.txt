A Fat Little Fellow (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    MASTER MEADOW MOUSE was pudgy. His legs were so short and his tail was  so short and his ears were so short that he looked even fatter than he  really was. And goodness knows he was plump enough--especially toward  fall when the corn was ripe.  He lived in Farmer Green's meadow. And he never harmed anybody.
