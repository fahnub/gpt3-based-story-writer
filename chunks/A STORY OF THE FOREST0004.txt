The Fir-tree spread her thick branches over them, and kept off the  fierce wind and the bitter cold, and under her shelter they were growing  strong.  They were all fine little trees, but one of them, that stood quite apart  from the rest, was the finest of all, very straight and well shaped  and handsome.
