If Mr. Chippy had known that his caller was going to be so rude he would  have stayed hidden in the wild grapevine. And now he wished that Jasper  would go away and leave him in peace. As for laughing, he saw nothing at  all to laugh at.  "You'd better do as I tell you!" Jasper Jay warned him. And he raised  his crest and stamped angrily upon the stone wall.
