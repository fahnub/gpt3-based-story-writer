The winged lion of Venice looked as if trying to fly away home;  the Chinese dragon appeared to brandish his forked tail as he clawed at  the Burmese peacock; the double-headed eagle of Russia pecked at the  Turkish crescent with one beak, while the other seemed to be screaming  to the English royal beast, "Come on and lend a paw."
