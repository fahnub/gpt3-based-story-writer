DOG SPOT'S PLANS (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    SOMEHOW old dog Spot and Miss Kitty Cat never became good friends. By  the time Miss Kitty Cat arrived on the farm in Pleasant Valley Spot had  lived there several years.  From the first day he met Miss Kitty in the kitchen Spot hadn't liked  her. Yet he claimed at the time that he was glad to see her. He said  that he could tell at once that he was going to have great sport with  her. He knew it would be fun to chase her!
