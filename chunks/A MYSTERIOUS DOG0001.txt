A MYSTERIOUS DOG (Fiction:- Ages 5 to 10)  The elm-tree avenue was all overgrown, the great gate was never  unlocked, and the old house had been shut up for several years.  Yet voices were heard about the place, the lilacs nodded over the high  wall as if they said, "We could tell fine secrets if we chose," and the  mullein outside the gate made haste to reach the keyhole, that it might  peep in and see what was going on.
