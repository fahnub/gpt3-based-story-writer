Bruno!" called  little Lola, and no Bruno came; but every frightened homesick little  doggy in that prison poked up his nose, wagged his tail, and started  for the voice. It didn't matter whether they were Fidos, or Carlos, or  Rovers, or Pontos; they knew that they were lonesome little dogs, and  perhaps somebody had remembered them.
