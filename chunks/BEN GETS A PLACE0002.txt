Sancho sat at the open window, watching the old cat wash her face, and  trying to imitate her with his great ruffled paw, so awkwardly that Ben  laughed; and Sanch, to hide his confusion at being caught, made one  bound from chair to bed, and licked his master's face so energetically  that the boy dived under the bedclothes to escape from the rough tongue.
