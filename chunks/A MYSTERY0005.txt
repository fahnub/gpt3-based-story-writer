And everybody except him said, "_Baa!_"  "He laughs best who laughs last," Aunt Nancy remarked to him. "To-morrow  we'll laugh best--at you!"  But Snowball stood his ground and shook his head.  "I'm not going to be sheared," he declared. "I guess you don't know what  Johnnie Green's 'to-morrow' means. . . . It means 'never!'"
