Thorny took turns to tell the few tragical  facts which were not shrouded in the deepest mystery. If the interesting  sufferer could only have spoken, what thrilling adventures and  hair-breadth escapes he might have related. But, alas! he was dumb; and  the secrets of that memorable month never were revealed.
