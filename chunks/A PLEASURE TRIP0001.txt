A PLEASURE TRIP (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    Almost always the wild folk in Pleasant Valley knew that if they wanted  to see Timothy Turtle they could find him somewhere in Black Creek. But  once in a great while he liked to go on what he called "an excursion."  By that he meant a pleasure trip to some spot not too far away--never  outside of Pleasant Valley.
