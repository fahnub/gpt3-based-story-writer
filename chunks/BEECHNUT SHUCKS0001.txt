BEECHNUT SHUCKS (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    ONCE in a while Frisky Squirrel paid a visit to Farmer Green's place.  Although he had learned that the farmyard was not without its dangers,  after one adventure Frisky was always sure to return, sometime, as if in  search of another.  So a certain fine, fall day found him scampering along the top of the  stone wall that followed the road as it dropped down the hill from the  woods to Farmer Green's front gate.
