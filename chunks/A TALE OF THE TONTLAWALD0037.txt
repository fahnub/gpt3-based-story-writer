Then she drew her golden comb gently through Elsa's hair, and bade her go to bed; but little sleep had the poor girl! Life seemed to stretch before her like a dark starless night.  Now let us look back a moment, and see what had been going on in Elsa's native village all these years, and how her double had fared.
