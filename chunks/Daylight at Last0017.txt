"I guess, partner, if the wuss comes  to the wuss, I could build a raft--or even a boat--from those trees,  so's we could sail away in it."  The little girl brightened at this suggestion. "I don't see the Ork  anywhere," she remarked, looking around. Then her eyes lighted upon  something and she exclaimed: "Oh, Cap'n Bill! Isn't that a house, over  there to the left?"
