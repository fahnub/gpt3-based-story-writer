BEN GETS A PLACE (Fiction:- Ages 5 to 10)  When Ben awoke next morning, he looked about him for a moment half  bewildered, because there was neither a canvas tent, a barn roof, nor  the blue sky above him, but a neat white ceiling, where several flies  buzzed sociably together, while from without came, not the tramping of  horses, the twitter of swallows, or the chirp of early birds, but the  comfortable cackle of hens and the sound of two little voices chanting  the multiplication table.
