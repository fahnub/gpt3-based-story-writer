There was something about the sound that sent a thrill through Nimble  and started his coat to bristling along his backbone with a queer,  creepy feeling.  One day in the fall Nimble's mother came upon them in the woods when  they were having one of their sham fights.  "You'd better stop that!" she said to them severely.
