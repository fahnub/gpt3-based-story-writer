"You look good to me."  And now it was Mr. Frog's turn to be pleased; for he was very vain.  "I'm glad to hear it!" he cried. "And I'll tell you a secret: I've  always been quite satisfied with myself until my eyes fell on you. Oh!  if I only had such a bill as yours!"  "You like my bill, then?" Mr. Heron asked him.
