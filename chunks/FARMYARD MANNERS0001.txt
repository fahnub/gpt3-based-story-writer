FARMYARD MANNERS (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    "Oh, very well!" said Simon Screecher to Turkey Proudfoot. "I'll give my  cousin your message. I'll tell him that you want him to meet you here in  this clearing in the woods to-morrow night." So off Simon Screecher  flew.  He had not been gone long when a noisy "_haw-haw-hoo-hoo_" rolled and  echoed through the woods.  "He's laughing!" Turkey Proudfoot exclaimed. "Solomon Owl is laughing. I  wonder what the joke is."
