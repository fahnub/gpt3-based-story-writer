FATTY COON GOES FISHING (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    One day Fatty Coon was strolling along the brook which flowed not far  from his home. He stopped now and then, to crouch close to the water's  edge, in the hope of catching a fish. And one time, when he lay quite  still among the rocks, at the side of a deep pool, with his eyes  searching the clear water, Fatty Coon suddenly saw something bright, all  yellow and red, that lighted on the water right before him.
