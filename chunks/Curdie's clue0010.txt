Nor had Curdie looked long before he recognized the king himself, and  found that he had made his way into the inner apartment of the royal  family.  He had never had such a good chance of hearing something.  He  crept through the hole as softly as he could, scrambled a good way down  the wall towards them without attracting attention, and then sat down  and listened.
