For, just as the evening star  was looking whitely through the rosy window of the west, Cecily came  flying through the orchard, wringing her hands.  "Oh, come, come quick," she gasped. "Dan's eating the bad berries  again--he's et a whole bunch of them--he says he'll show Felicity. I  can't stop him. Come you and try."  We rose in a body and rushed towards the house.
