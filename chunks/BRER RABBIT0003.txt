Now, ol' Brer B'ar wuz a-settin' in de cheer, suh,  So he stand up an' move a motion;   He up an' 'low, "Le's erso'v right here, suh,  Fer ter thank Brer Buzzard whiles we're in de notion,   An' not put it off ter some yuther day."  An' den dey had it up an' down, suh,  'Sputin' 'bout what dey oughter do,   Some wanter gi' 'im a flower crown, suh,  Ef he rid Brer Rabbit up dar in de blue,   An' drap 'im when he got half-way.
