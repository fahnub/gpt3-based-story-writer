The air was filled  with falling white flakes, so large in size and so queer in form that  she was puzzled.  "Are you certain this is snow?" she asked.  "To be sure. I must get my snow-shovel and turn out to shovel a path.  Would you like to come with me?"  "Yes," she said, and followed the Bumpy Man out when he opened the  door. Then she exclaimed: "Why, it isn't cold a bit!"
