CHASING MISS KITTY (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    WHEN Miss Kitty Cat awoke from her nap she got up and stretched herself.  In her opinion, a nap was no nap at all if one didn't stretch after  taking it. "There's nothing like a good stretch to make a person  limber," she often remarked.  Of course, in order to climb trees, or spring successfully at a rat or a  mouse, Miss Kitty had to keep her muscles supple.
