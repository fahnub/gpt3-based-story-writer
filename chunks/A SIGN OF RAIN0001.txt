A SIGN OF RAIN (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    OVER near the garden fence lay an old hollow log. Grandfather Mole  discovered it one day; and thinking that it would be a fine place to  look for grubs and other good things, he crept into one end of it.  If he had been able to see, near the other end of the log, a pair of  bright eyes that peered at him out of the darkness perhaps he would have  backed out in a hurry. But it was all right.
