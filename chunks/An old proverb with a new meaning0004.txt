"It isn't ANY use for me to try to learn to cook," she said.  "Never mind," I said consolingly. "You can tell splendid stories."  "But what good would that do a hungry boy?" wailed the Story Girl.  "Boys ain't ALWAYS hungry," said Felix gravely. "There's times when they  ain't."  "I don't believe it," said the Story Girl drearily.
