When it was finished he bored a hole in the doll's breast and put a bit of bread inside; then, drawing a snake out of the basket, forced it to enter the hollow body.  'Now,' he said to the lady, 'all we want is a drop of the maiden's blood.'  When she heard this Elsa grew white with horror, for she thought she was selling her soul to the evil one.
