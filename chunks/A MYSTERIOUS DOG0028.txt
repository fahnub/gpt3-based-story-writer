Hardly had  they recovered from this shock, when the hind-legs came down, the  fore-legs went up, and he paraded in a soldierly manner to and fro, like  a sentinel on guard. But the crowning performance was when he took his  tail in his mouth and waltzed down the walk, over the prostrate dolls,  to the gate and back again, barely escaping a general upset of the  ravaged table.
