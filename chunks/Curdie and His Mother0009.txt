'But what I am going to tell you is very strange,' persisted his  mother; 'and if having heard it you were to say I must have been  dreaming, I don't know that I should have any right to be vexed with  you, though I know at least that I was not asleep.'  'Do tell me, mother.  Perhaps it will help me to think better of the  princess.'  'That's why I am tempted to tell you,' replied his mother.
