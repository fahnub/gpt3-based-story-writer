Kiddie found Benjamin Bat a much more agreeable  companion when he had eaten his fill. Early in the evening, soon after  he had waked up, Benjamin was positively ferocious. But the more he ate,  the pleasanter he grew. And by the time faint streaks of light began to  show in the east he could smile and crack a joke as easily as anybody  else.
