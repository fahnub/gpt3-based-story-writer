Mouse was never so lazy as that. And the day  came at last when it was well worth his while to take the little extra  trouble of peeping out before he had his swim.  For Master Meadow Mouse caught a glimpse of a snakelike head that darted  out from under the bank of the brook and darted back again, out of  sight. He knew that that queer head belonged to Peter Mink, and to  nobody else.  [Illustration]      [Illustration]
