Aunt Olivia assented  lightly, swinging her hat on her arm and including us all in a friendly  smile. She looked very pretty, with her big blue eyes and warm-hued  golden hair. We loved Aunt Olivia; but just now we resented her having  laughed at us with Aunt Janet, and we refused to smile back.  "What a sulky, sulky lot of little people," said Aunt Olivia, going away  across the yard, holding her pretty dress up from the dewy grass.
