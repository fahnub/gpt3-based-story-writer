No matter what anybody else said, he solemnly hurled back  at him that neverending refrain, _Katy did, Katy did; she did, she did!_  You would have thought, if you had heard Kiddie, that somebody had  disputed his statement. But such was not the case at all. Since no one  except the Katydids knew anything about the mysterious Katy, nobody was  able to say truthfully that she _didn't_ do it.
