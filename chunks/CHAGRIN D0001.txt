CHAGRIN D'AMOUR (Poetry:- Ages 3 to 5)      IF Love and I were all alone    I might forget to grieve,    And for his pleasure and my own    Might happier garlands weave;    But you sit there, and watch us wear    The mourning wreaths you wove:    And while such mocking eyes you bear    I am not friends with Love.
