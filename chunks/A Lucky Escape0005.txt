And while he was searching, the  deserted raft swung off down the creek, hung for a few moments at the  edge of the channel, and then drifted lazily toward shore, where it  lodged at last among the reeds.  The disappointed fisherman returned to his fishing. But it seemed as if  his luck had turned. Not another fish came his way.
