"AND THE RAINS DESCENDED AND THE FLOODS CAME (Poetry:- Ages 3 to 5)  "    NOW the far waves roll nearer and more near,    The wind's awake, the pitiless wind's awake,    It shrieks the menace that I dare not hear,    Soon at my feet the angry waves will break    In desolating wrath--and here I stand    Helpless my house is built upon the sand.      O you, whose house upon a rock is set,    Laugh, safe and sure, at threatening wave and wind.    You chose the better part and yet--and yet,    There
