And the Phoenix replied, this time in English, 'Why are you sad so nearChristmas-time?'  The children looked at it with one gasp of horror and surprise, forthe youngest of them knew that it is far from manners to notice thatstrangers have been crying, and much worse to ask them the reason oftheir tears.
