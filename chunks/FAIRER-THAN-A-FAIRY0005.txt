Several years passed, during which time the Princess grew accustomed to  her lonely life, obeyed the Fairy's orders, and by degrees forgot all  about the court of the King her father.  One day, whilst passing near a fountain in the garden, she noticed  that the sun's rays fell on the water in such a manner as to produce  a brilliant rainbow.
