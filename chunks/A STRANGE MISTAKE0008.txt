Although Long Bill Wren continued to laugh heartily, neither Mr.  Frog nor Rusty could manage even a faint smile. Having expected a  merry time and plenty to eat, they were both disappointed.  But Mr. Frog soon said that so far as he was concerned, he still  had a singing-party that he could attend, so he didn't feel sad  very long.
