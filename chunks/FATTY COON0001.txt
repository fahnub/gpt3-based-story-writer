And you may think it strange when  I tell you that not one of the squirrels anywhere around Blue Mountain  was the least bit fond of Fatty Coon. But when I say that Fatty Coon was  fond of squirrels, I mean that he liked to eat them. So of course you  will understand now why the squirrels did not care for Fatty at all.
