When Snowball  caught his first glimpse of the young black ram, after Farmer Green had  sheared him and turned him back into the pasture, minus his fleece,  Snowball did not know him. Just for a moment Snowball thought the young  black ram was a new kind of dog.  "Old dog Spot won't care for this stranger," Snowball thought. He was  about to warn the stranger to leave the farm at once, when he saw that  he wasn't a dog after all.
