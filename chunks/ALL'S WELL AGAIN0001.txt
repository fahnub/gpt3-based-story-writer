ALL'S WELL AGAIN (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    After Rusty Wren had revived his drooping spirits by eating  heartily of three dozen insects of different kinds and sizes, he  felt so cheerful that he couldn't help trilling a few songs. It was  almost evening; and he was glad not to let the sun go down without  thanking him in that way for shining so brightly all day.
