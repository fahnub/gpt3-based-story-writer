From the gate to the porch went a wide walk, paved with smooth slabs of  dark stone, and bordered with the tall bushes which met overhead, making  a green roof. All sorts of neglected flowers and wild weeds grew between  their stems, covering the walls of this summer parlor with the prettiest  tapestry.
