And off he went, to find Henrietta Hen and tell  her how he and Turkey Proudfoot were going to help each other.  "You're even sillier than I supposed," she informed the rooster, to his  great astonishment. He had expected nothing but praise from her.  He left her hurriedly. And he felt quite glum.
