A PLEASANT HOME (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Now, Rusty Wren had found--and shown to his wife--a hollow apple  tree and a hole in a fence-rail, either of which he thought would  make a pleasant place in which to live.  But since the little couple were _house wrens_, Rusty's wife said  she thought that they oughtn't to be so far from the farmhouse.  "Why not build our nest behind one of the shutters?" she  suggested.  But Rusty shook his head quickly--and with decision.  "That won't do," said he.
