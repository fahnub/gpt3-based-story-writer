AN UNWELCOME GUEST (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    AS he neared his home in the cherry tree, Rusty Wren saw a fearsome  sight. Miss Kitty Cat was crouched right on top of the tin syrup can  which Johnnie Green had nailed to the tree. Inside that can was the Wren  family's nest. And inside the nest were some brand-new youngsters, only  two days out of their shells.  It was no wonder that when Rusty Wren came hack from the orchard and saw  such a sight he began to shriek.
