A JOKE ON JASPER JAY (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    WITH a loud squall of glee, Jasper Jay made off in the direction of the  farm buildings. Now that he was going to have company, later, he felt  much better. And he resolved to keep well hidden in the top of the great  oak near Farmer Green's house, until the time came for Mr. Crow to  arrive--and his friends, too, if he brought them.
