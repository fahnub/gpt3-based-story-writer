Cerberus (Poetry / Picture Book Ages 3 to 5)    Dear Reader, should you chance to go  To Hades, do not fail to throw  A "Sop to Cerberus" at the gate,  His anger to propitiate.  Don't say "Good dog!" and hope thereby  His three fierce Heads to pacify.  What though he try to be polite  And wag his Tail with all his might,  How shall one amiable Tail  Against three angry Heads prevail?
