A GREAT FLURRY(Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    There was a great flurry among Farmer Green's hens. They all insisted on  talking at the same time, because they had heard an astonishing bit of  news. It was about Henrietta Hen. Wherever she went her neighbors craned  their necks at her, just as if they hadn't seen her every day for as long  as they could remember.
