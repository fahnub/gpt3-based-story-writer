A STROKE OF LUCK (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    JASPER'S fun would have been spoiled if he hadn't had a stroke of good  fortune. Since he was no longer leading the nutting party he wanted to  prevent his friends from following Noisy Jake to the place where the oak  trees grew, to have an acorn hunt.  It was no more than anybody could expect that Jasper should feel sulky.  It had been his party in the first place. So, of course, he didn't enjoy  seeing somebody else take the lead away from him.
