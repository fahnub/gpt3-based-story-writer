Meanwhile the birds were all exclaiming, "There! He's here. Now Miss  Kitty Cat had better watch out."  Again a strange, mocking catcall sounded from somewhere. There was a  sort of jeer about it that aroused Miss Kitty Cat's anger.  "He's come, has he?" she exclaimed to little Mr. Chippy, who chattered  at her from a good, safe distance.
