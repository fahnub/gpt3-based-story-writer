For the moment the last angry  Bumblebee had disappeared inside it Johnnie Green stole quickly up from  behind a haycock and slipped the cork into the mouth of the jug.  Johnnie's face wore a grin of joy. Perhaps he did not stop to realize  that he was breaking up a happy home.  "I've got 'em!" he shouted aloud.
