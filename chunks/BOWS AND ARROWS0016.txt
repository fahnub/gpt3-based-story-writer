Kilburn caught up his gun with one hand and his little girl  with the other, and ran as fast as he could toward the fort. But before  he could reach it he heard a yell, and saw the red men coming up from  the river. Then he knew it would be in vain to try to get in, so he  looked about for a safe place to hide Matty till he could come for her.  He was a brave man, and could fight, so he had no thought of hiding  while his neighbors needed help; but the dear little daughter must be  cared for
