Bab's turn came first; and, as Miss Celia examined her bow  to see that all was right, the little girl said, With her eyes on her  rival's excited face,--  "I want to beat, but Ben will feel so bad, I 'most hope I sha'n't."  "Losing a prize sometimes makes one happier than gaining it. You have  proved that you could do better than most of them; so, if you do not  beat, you may still feet proud," answered Miss Celia, giving back the  bow with a smile that said more than her words.
