A QUEER TOADSTOOL (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Mr. Crow did exactly as he said he would. After the time he was caught  out in the shower and got wet he never went even the shortest distance  away from home without his umbrella. And he wouldn't even let anybody  take his umbrella, in order to look at it.  "It might rain suddenly," Mr. Crow explained.
