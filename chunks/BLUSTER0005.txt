The rooster had always acted afraid of him. Though small, the rooster  was strongly built. And he had a sharp bill and sharp spurs, too. Turkey  Proudfoot noted these details carefully.  "I won't have to fight him," he thought. "I'll behave so fiercely that  the rooster will be glad to run off. And then I'll run after him so  folks will think I am chasing him."
