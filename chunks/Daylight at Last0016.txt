Cap'n Bill and Trot decided to walk through the forest, to discover  what was on the other side of it, but the Ork's feet were still so sore  and "lumpy" from walking on the rocks that the creature said he  preferred to fly over the tree-tops and meet them on the other side.  The forest was not large, so by walking briskly for fifteen minutes  they reached its farthest edge and saw before them the shore of the  ocean.  "It's an island, all right," said Trot, with a sigh.
