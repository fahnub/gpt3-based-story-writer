BEN'S BIRTHDAY (Fiction:- Ages 5 to 10)  A superb display of flags flapped gayly in the breeze on the September  morning when Ben proudly entered his teens. An irruption of bunting  seemed to have broken out all over the old house, for banners of every  shape and size, color and design, flew from chimney-top to gable, porch  and gate-way, making the quiet place look as lively as a circus tent,  which was just what Ben most desired and delighted in.
