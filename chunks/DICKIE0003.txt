But none of the birds cared for it at all, except Long Bill Wren; and  even he remarked that the house would be better "if it was rounder."  As for Jasper Jay, he told Dickie Deer Mouse that, in his opinion, the  house was ruined.  "It's nothing but a trap," he declared. "And I'd hate to go to sleep  inside it."
