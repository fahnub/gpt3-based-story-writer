It was rather foggy, and we waited  a good while beside the railings, but all the belated travellers were  either grown up or else they were Board School children. We weren't  going to get into a row with grown-up people--especially strangers--and  no true bandit would ever stoop to ask a ransom from the relations of  the poor and needy.
