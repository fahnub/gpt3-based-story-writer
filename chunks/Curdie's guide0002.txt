Without saying a word, for he knew no  one would believe him any more than he had believed the princess, he  followed the thread with his finger, contrived to give Lootie the slip,  and was soon out of the house and on the mountainside--surprised that,  if the thread were indeed the grandmother's messenger, it should have  led the princess, as he supposed it must, into the mountain, where she  would be certain to meet the goblins rushing back enraged from their  defeat.
