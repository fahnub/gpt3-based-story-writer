She advised them always for the best, and even offered her services to dress their hair, which they were very willing she should do.  As she was doing this, they said to her:--  "Cinderella, would you not be glad to go to the ball?"  "Young ladies," she said, "you only jeer at me; it is not for such as I am to go there."
