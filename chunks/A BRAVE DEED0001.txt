A BRAVE DEED (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    Nobody had ever supposed that the Muley Cow had much courage. In many  ways she seemed quite timid. Perhaps if she had had horns she would have  been different. Anyhow, whenever anything startled her the only thing  she ever did was to run away, if she could.
