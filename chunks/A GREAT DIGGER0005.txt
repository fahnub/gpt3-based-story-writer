They were sharp. And so, too, were his teeth,which he never hesitated to use in a fight.  Left alone, Benny Badger--when he wasn't too hungry--was a peaceableperson. But if a dog ever tried to worry him Benny had a most unpleasantway of seizing his annoyer with his powerful jaws and holding the poorcreature as if he never intended to let him go.
