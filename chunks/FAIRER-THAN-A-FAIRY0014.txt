Fairer-than-a-Fairy eagerly ran to the window, but in her haste she  upset the basin, and spilt all the water with which she had carefully  filled it overnight. No other water was at hand except that in the two  bottles. It was the only chance of seeing her lover before they were  separated, and she did not hesitate to break the bottle and pour their  contents into the basin, when the Rainbow appeared at once.
