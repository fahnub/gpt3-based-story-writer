Owing to the rain we could not foregather in the orchard, where we had  meant to transact the business with Jerry. We did not wish our grown-ups  around at our great moment, so we betook ourselves to the loft of the  granary in the spruce wood, from whose window we could see the main road  and hail Jerry.
