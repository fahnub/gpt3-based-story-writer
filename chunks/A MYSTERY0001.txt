A MYSTERY (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    As Snowball drew near the pasture bars he forgot about the blow on the  head that the black ram had given him. The strange sights that greeted  his eyes drove all unpleasant things out of his mind.  Snowball knew that the sheep he saw before him must be his old  companions.
