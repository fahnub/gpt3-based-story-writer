Miss Kitty Cat just naturally began to think of her own  breakfast.  "If I were at home, Mrs. Green would be setting a saucer of milk on the  woodshed steps about this time," she murmured. "But now I must shift for  myself."  Luckily Miss Kitty was quite able to find something to eat, as a  surprised meadow mouse soon discovered.
