Before very long the ice between Trot and the queen had melted away  entirely, and with a cry of joy the little girl flopped her pink  tail and swam to the side of her deliverer.  "Are you very cold?" asked Aquareine.  "N-not v-v-very!" replied Trot, but her teeth chattered and she was  still shivering.  "The water will be warm in a few minutes," said the Queen.
