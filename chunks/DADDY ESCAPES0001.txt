DADDY ESCAPES (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    THAT was a fine idea of General Antenna's--that plan of bringing  stepladders, so that the ant army might climb up on them and reach Daddy  Longlegs, whom they were attacking.  And Captain Kidd told the General on the spot that it was a most happy  thought.  "Then do as I tell you, at once!" the General ordered pompously.  "I can't!" said Captain Kidd, who was terribly frightened, because the  General was sure to be angry.  "_Can't!_" cried General Antenna
