A Handy Sign (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    HUNTING played a great part in the life of Master Meadow Mouse. Somebody  or other was always hunting him. And he was always hunting for something  to eat. He spent a good deal of his time away from home, looking for  seeds and grain. On the other hand, he spent a good deal of his time in  his house; for Master Meadow Mouse liked to take naps--especially in the  daytime.
