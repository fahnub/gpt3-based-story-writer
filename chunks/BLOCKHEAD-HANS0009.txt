'Why!' said the brothers, 'this is pure mud, straight from the ditch.'  'Of course it is!' said Blockhead-Hans, 'and it is the best kind! Look  how it runs through one's fingers!' and, so saying, he filled his pocket  with the mud.  But the brothers rode on so fast that dust and sparks flew all around,  and they reached the gate of the town a good hour before Blockhead-Hans.
