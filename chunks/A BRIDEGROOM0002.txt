"Goodness!" said Buster Bumblebee. "He looks like a bridegroom!" But  his companions all told him to hush, and not to be disrespectful to his  elders.  "Good morning!" said Mr. Chippy to Daddy Longlegs. "We've come to tell  you some good news. We're going to call you by a different name. And we  hope you'll like it."  "I hope so!" Daddy Longlegs echoed. "What is it, please?"  "It's 'Grandfather Graybeard'!" little Mr. Chippy informed him with an  air of pride.  "Why, that's a beautiful name!"
