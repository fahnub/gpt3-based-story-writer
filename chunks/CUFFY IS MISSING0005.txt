And everybody knew  that Peter Mink had no home at all! He was the vagabond of the woods.  Nimble saw then that it was useless to look for help from him. And after  Peter Mink had gone his surly way Nimble still lingered there. He was  hungry. So he began to paw the snow away here and there, to uncover the  ground growths.
