"Come, let's join hands!" cried Aqua; and so they all dashed on together  till they came to a broad river which opened its arms to them.  By the help of Aqua and his brothers the beautiful river was able to  float heavy ships, though not so long ago it was only a little rill,  through which a child could wade or over which he could step.
