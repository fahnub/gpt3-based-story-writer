DESPAIR (Poetry:- Ages 3 to 5)      SMILE on me, mouth of red--so much too red,    Shine on me, eyes which darkened lashes shade,    Turn, turn my way, oh glorious golden head,    My soul is lost, then let the price be paid!    Amid rich flowers your rosy lamplight gleams,    Amid rich hangings pass your scented hours,    And woods and fields are green but in my dreams,    And only in my dreams grow meadow-flowers.
