And, in less time than it takes to tell it, he was  perched on top of his house again and calling to his wife.  "I know now where the tobacco came from!" he sang out. "Just come  outside and I'll show you. It's upstairs in the carriage house!"  To his delight, Mrs. Rusty answered in the sweetest tone  imaginable. But she said she didn't want to come out just then.
