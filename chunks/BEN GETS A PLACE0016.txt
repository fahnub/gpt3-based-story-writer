Squire's horse was a joke  all over the town, being about twenty years old, and having a peculiar  gait of his own, lifting his fore-feet very high, with a great show of  speed, though never going out of a jog-trot. The boys used to say he  galloped before and walked behind, and made all sorts of fun of the big,  Roman-nosed beast, who allowed no liberties to be taken with him.
