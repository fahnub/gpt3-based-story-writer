Well, Johnnie Green knew that when his father talked like that it was  silly to be glum. So he cried, "All right!" And turning his back upon  the black lamb, which was by this time almost up to the head of the  lane, Johnnie walked back to the woodshed.  The next day, when Farmer Green came home from a drive over the hill,  Johnnie shouted "Hurrah!" once more.
