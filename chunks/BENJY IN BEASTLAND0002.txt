They were dear, merry little things, and many boys  would have found them pleasant little playmates; but Benjy had shown how  much he disliked to play with them, and it made them feel very badly.  One of them said one day, "Benjy does not care for us because we are  only girls, so we have taken Nox for our brother." Nox was a big curly  dog, something like a Newfoundland.
