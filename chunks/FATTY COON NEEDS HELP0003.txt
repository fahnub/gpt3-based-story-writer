So instead of quitting his snug home--as he had feared he must--he went  back to it to finish his nap.  Now, Dickie Deer Mouse had lost so much sleep--through being disturbed  by Mr. Crow and Jasper Jay--that when night came he kept right on  sleeping. Yes!
