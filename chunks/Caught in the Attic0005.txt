Squirrel was really frightened. The window was shut!  You see, while Frisky was so busy eating butternuts, a storm was  gathering. And it grew so dark, and the wind howled so shrilly, that  Farmer Green's wife thought she had better shut the attic window, to  keep the rain from beating in.  How Frisky Squirrel did wish he had minded his mother and kept away  from old Mr. Crow! Poor Frisky looked out through the little square  panes of glass. His friend Mr. Crow was nowhere to be seen.
