Dorothy hastened to introduce them to the assembled company.  They were  the first foreign arrivals, and the friends of Princess Ozma were  polite to them and tried to make them feel that they were welcome.  Chick the Cherub shook hands with every one, including Billina, and was  so joyous and frank and full of good spirits that John Dough's Head  Booleywag at once became a prime favorite.
