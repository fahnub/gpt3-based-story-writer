BRER RABBIT FINDS THE MOON IN THE MILL POND (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)   [Illustration]    Oh, one bright day in de middle er May,   Brer Rabbit wuz feelin' fine;   He tuck ter de road, an' never know'd   De place whar he wuz gwine!  "Oh, fur an' free," sezee, "siree,   No gal kin change my min'!"   Brer Tarrypin, sly, he wunk one eye,   Un'neat' his green-gourd vine!
