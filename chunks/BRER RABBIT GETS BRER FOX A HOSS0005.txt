An' den an' dar, Brer Rabbit 'gun ter snicker,   Wid, "Hol' 'im, Brer Fox! 'twon't do ter flicker!   Ef you make 'im stan' still, you kin ride 'im de quicker!"   De hoss, he r'ar'd an' raise a mighty dust up,   An' fust thing you know, Brer Rabbit hear a bust-up!   "I hope, Brer Fox, dat you ain't much hurt--   But yo' wife'll be mad, kaze you done tored yo' shirt!"
