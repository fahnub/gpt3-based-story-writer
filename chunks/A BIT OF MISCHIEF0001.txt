A BIT OF MISCHIEF (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    JASPER JAY did not heed Mr. Crow's warning. When he learned that Mr.  Red-shouldered Hawk was angry with him because he had imitated Mr.  Hawk's fierce cry, "_Kee-you! kee-you!_" Jasper was more pleased with  himself than ever. Scaring Farmer Green's hens with that piercing scream  had been a good deal of fun. But making Mr. Hawk angry was still more.  So Jasper Jay began to visit the farmyard even oftener than before.
