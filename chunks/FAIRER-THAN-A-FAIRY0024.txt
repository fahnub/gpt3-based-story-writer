The Princess climbed up one of the silver chains, holding in her hand  the little porter who, in spite of his minute size, opened a secret door  with his golden key and let her in. She entered a magnificent room which  appeared to occupy the entire Castle, and which was lighted by gold and  jewelled stars in the ceiling.
