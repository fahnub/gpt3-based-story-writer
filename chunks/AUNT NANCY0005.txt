Well, that thought was enough to make Snowball swerve sharply to his  right. And a few moments later he bobbed over the wall a little further  up the hillside.  Just beyond the wall grew a tangle of berry bushes. And into the midst  of them Snowball jumped. And out of the midst of them, right in front of  him, there rose up on his hind legs--a bear!
