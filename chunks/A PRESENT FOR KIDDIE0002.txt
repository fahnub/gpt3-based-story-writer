"Mr. Crow will be very angry with us to-morrow when he learns we haven't  called on Kiddie Katydid," somebody remarked. And a hush fell upon the  company. But Chirpy Cricket had a happy thought, which made them all  feel better.  "Kiddie Katydid won't stay out of doors on a night like this!" he  suddenly exclaimed.
