Dorothy Receives the Guests (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)      Next morning Dorothy's breakfast was served in her own pretty sitting  room, and she sent to invite Polly and the shaggy man to join her and  Button-Bright at the meal.  They came gladly, and Toto also had  breakfast with them, so that the little party that had traveled  together to Oz was once more reunited.
