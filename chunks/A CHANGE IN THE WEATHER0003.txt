"It'll be good and cold by night," said Mr. Frog. "I look for a sudden  change in the weather. Nobody ought to venture out to-night without his  heaviest overcoat on."  After flinging that remark over his shoulder, Mr. Frog flung himself  inside his tailor's shop and slammed the door behind him.
