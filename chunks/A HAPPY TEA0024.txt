Bab seized the games, and Ben was lost in admiration of the  little Arab chief prancing on the white horse,--all saddled and  bridled and fit for the fight. Thorny poked about to find a certain  curious puzzle which he could put together without a mistake after long  study. Even Sancho found something to interest him; and, standing on his  hind-legs, thrust his head between the boys to paw at several red and  blue letters on square blocks.
