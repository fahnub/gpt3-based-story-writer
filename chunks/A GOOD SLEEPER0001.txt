A GOOD SLEEPER (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Twinkleheels' stall was an end one. Next to him stood the old horse  Ebenezer; and beyond Ebenezer were the two bays. Twinkleheels often  wished that he might have someone for his nearest neighbor that was a  bit livelier than Ebenezer. When the old horse stayed in the barn he  spent a great deal of his time with his eyes half shut, dozing. If  Twinkleheels spoke to him, Ebenezer seldom heard him the first time.
