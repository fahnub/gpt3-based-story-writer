A NEAT HOUSEKEEPER (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Rusty Wren's wife was a very neat housekeeper. Every day she  carefully cleaned her house, chirping while she worked. Sometimes  her voice was sweet and pleasant. But at other times--though it was  still sweet--it was not pleasant at all. And whenever Rusty heard  that second kind of chirp he was always careful to find some errand  that took him away from home.  You see, Rusty Wren was not so orderly as his wife.
