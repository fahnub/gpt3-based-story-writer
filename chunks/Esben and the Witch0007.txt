As soon as shehad gone her way Esben wakened his brothers, and they hastily took theirhorses and rode off from the witch's house, glad that they had escapedso well. They quite forgot to thank Esben for what he had done for them.  When they had ridden onwards for some time they reached a king's palace,and inquired there whether they could be taken into service.
