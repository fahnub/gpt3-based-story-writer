BILLY, BILLY (Rhymes:- Ages 0 to 3)  "Billy, Billy, come and play,  While the sun shines bright as day."    "Yes, my Polly, so I will,  For I love to please you still."    "Billy, Billy, have you seen  Sam and Betsy on the green?"    "Yes, my Poll, I saw them pass,  Skipping o'er the new-mown grass."    "Billy, Billy, come along,  And I will sing a pretty song."
