But vainly did she risk her neck jumping off the highest beams in the  barn, trying to keep her balance standing on the donkey's back, and  leaping the lodge gate at a bound; Ben vouchsafed no reward by a look, a  smile, a word of commendation; and Bab felt that nothing but Sancho's  return would ever restore the broken friendship.
