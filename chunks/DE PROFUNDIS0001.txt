DE PROFUNDIS (Poetry:- Ages 3 to 5)      NOW I am cast into the serpent pit    And, catching difficult breath    From the writhing, loathsome, ceaseless stir of it,    The venomous whispers of curling, clasping Death,    I lift my soul out of the pit to Thee    And reaching with my soul to where Thou art    Look down, seeing with free heart    The beast God gave my soul for company    Lie with companions fit;    And bid, with a good will,    The serpent-fangs of ill    Take their foul fill    Of
