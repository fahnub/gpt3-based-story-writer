A FRIEND, INDEED (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    The next morning Rusty Wren awakened with a start. Somebody was  pounding at his door--and shouting his name, as well. He jumped out  of bed to see what was the matter. And, looking outside, he beheld  Mr. Chippy, with sixteen of his cousins, all very much excited--if  one might judge by their actions.
