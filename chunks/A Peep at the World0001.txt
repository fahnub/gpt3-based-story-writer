A Peep at the World (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    WHAT is the earliest thing you can remember? Master Meadow Mouse's  earliest memory was of lying in a soft nest of dried grasses. Sometimes  the nest was in inky darkness; and then it was night. Sometimes a shaft  of light fell upon the nest through a round hole just above his head;  and then it was daytime.
