Curdie's Clue (Fiction:- Ages 5 to 10)  Curdie was as watchful as ever, but was almost getting tired of his ill  success.  Every other night or so he followed the goblins about, as  they went on digging and boring, and getting as near them as he could,  watched them from behind stones and rocks; but as yet he seemed no  nearer finding out what they had in view.
