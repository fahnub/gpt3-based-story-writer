But the fires were  out, now; and Fatty was in no danger of being burned.  The hams were what Fatty Coon had smelled. And the hams were what Fatty  intended to eat. He decided that he would eat them all--though of course  he could never have done that--at least, not in one night; nor in a  week, either.
