"We may as well tell you," they said to Henrietta Hen, "that that chap  out there is a duckling. The water won't hurt him."  Henrietta Hen gasped and gaped. She was astonished. But she soon pulled  herself together. And it was just like her to begin to boast.  "See!" she cried to her friends, and waved a wing toward the water with  an air of pride.
