"I think this is all my news, except that I want to tell you how much  we all think of you, Peter. When we heard you were sick we all said nice  things about you, but we were afraid it was too late, and I said if you  got better I'd tell you. It is easier to write it than to tell it out  to your face. We think you are smart and polite and obliging and a great  worker and a gentleman.  "Your true friend,  "CECILY KING.  "P.S.
