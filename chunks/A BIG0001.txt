A BIG, FAT POTATO (Poetry:- Ages 0 to 3)      A big, fat potato lay down on a clod    In the shade of some burdock and tall goldenrod,    And he dreamed he were king of the whole garden plot,    With a palace and throne, and a crown with a lot    Of jewels and diamonds and gold till it shone    Like the front of a show when the lights are turned on.
