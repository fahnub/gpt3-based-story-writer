Jimmy Rabbit was becoming uneasy. And at last he spoke.  "I thought," he said, "you told me you had come down here to try the  carrots."  "So I did," Nimble answered. "But I don't know where the carrots are."  "Why didn't you say so before?" Jimmy Rabbit asked him. And without  waiting for a reply he cried, "Follow me! I'll show you."
