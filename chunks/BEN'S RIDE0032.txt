Then she shut her eyes, and looked so white that Ben hurried away, and  burst upon old Mrs. Paine, placidly knitting at the end door, so  suddenly that, as she afterward said, "It sca't her like a clap o'  thunder."  "Ain't a man nowheres around.  All down in the big medder gettin' in  hay," was her reply to Ben's breathless demand for "everybody to come  and see to Miss Celia."
