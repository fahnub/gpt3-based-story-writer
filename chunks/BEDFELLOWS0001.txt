BEDFELLOWS (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    During his rambles on the following night Dickie Deer Mouse took great  care to keep out of sight of the three families of cousins that had  tried to quarter themselves in his new house in the pasture. Moreover he  said nothing to anybody about his future home. Fatty Coon had taught him  in one lesson that it is sometimes wise to keep a secret.
