different way. Jasper Jay always wore a  brilliant blue suit, while Reddy Woodpecker made himself easily seen by  donning a bright red cap, which came down to his shoulders and gave him  an odd look. Being so much alike (as far as manners were concerned), the  two quarreled whenever they met. And when Jasper Jay heard that Reddy  had made up his mind to spend the winter in the North he was furious.  "It's an outrage!" he declared to Jimmy Rabbit, who had told him about  Reddy Woodpecker's plan.
