Miss Kitty was out of the buttery. Anyhow, it was very  plain to Mrs. Green that she had been in the buttery only a moment  before, lapping thick cream off a pan of milk. And she hadn't had time  to wash her face.  After that Farmer Green stopped up the stove-pipe hole. And soon Miss  Kitty's appetite for milk returned.
