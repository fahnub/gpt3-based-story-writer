All fear, or desire to go home, or to risk either schooling or a thrashing, passed away also.  Into a dark passage all three went, but they soon came out into a beautiful country. How the birds sang and the flowers bloomed! All around could be heard the joyful shouts of little folks at play. Never did things look so lovely.  [Illustration: THE KING SPOKE
