CRACKED CORN (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    The next time Johnnie Green dragged Snowball into the farmyard he shut  the gate carefully behind him.  "We'll never join the circus if you're going to behave like this,"  Johnnie told Snowball severely. "Now, you pay attention!"  He held up a bare hoop--not a paper-covered one--and when he said,  "Jump!" Snowball showed that he had not forgotten his lesson of the  afternoon before.  "That's better!" cried Johnnie Green.
