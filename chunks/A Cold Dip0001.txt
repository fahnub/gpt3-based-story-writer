A Cold Dip (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    IN one way Peter Mink was like Master Meadow Mouse. He enjoyed swimming.  And he spent a great deal of his time along the streams that threaded  their way through Pleasant Valley. Sometimes Peter dawdled on the banks  of Swift River. Sometimes he lingered for days in the neighborhood of  Black Creek. Nor did he disdain so small a stream as the brook that  crossed the meadow. It was deep enough for a swim.
