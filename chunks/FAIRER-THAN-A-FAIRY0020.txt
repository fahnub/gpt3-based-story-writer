As the green and white lady took leave of the Princess she gave her a  nut, desiring her only to open it in the most urgent need.  After a long and tiring journey Fairer-than-a-Fairy was once more  received in a house, and by a lady exactly like the one she had quitted.  Here again she received a present with the same injunctions, but instead  of a nut this lady gave her a golden pomegranate.
