Keep mourning watch with us. This is the hour    When Love lends all his power    To speed grief's arrows from the bow of Death,    When sighs are idle breath,    When tears are fountains vain.    She will not wake again,    Not now, not here.    O great and good and infinitely dear,    O Mother of your people, sleep is sweet,    No more Life's thorny ways will wound your feet.
