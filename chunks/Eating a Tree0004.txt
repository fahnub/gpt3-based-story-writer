And then he hurried away again.  "I wish he'd wait a minute," Master Meadow Mouse grumbled as he tore  after his cousin once more. "I don't feel like running. I haven't had a  hearty meal for days."  The cousin seemed surprised when Master Meadow Mouse overtook him.  "What!" that busy gentleman exclaimed.
