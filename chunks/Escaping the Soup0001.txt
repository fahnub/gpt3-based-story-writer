Escaping the Soup-Kettle (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)      The shaggy man got up and felt of himself to see if he was hurt; but he  was not.  One of the heads had struck his breast and the other his left  shoulder; yet though they had knocked him down, the heads were not hard  enough to bruise him.  "Come on," he said firmly; "we've got to get out of here some way," and  forward he started again.  The Scoodlers began yelling and throwing their heads in great numbers  at our frightened friends.
