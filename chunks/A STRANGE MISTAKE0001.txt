A STRANGE MISTAKE (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    Not wishing to be late at his cousin's party, which he understood  was to begin at five o'clock, Rusty Wren hurried along the bank of  Black Creek, while Mr. Frog did his best to keep pace with him.  Somewhat out of breath, the two arrived shortly at the home of Long  Bill Wren. And, to their surprise, they saw not the least sign of  any other guests.
