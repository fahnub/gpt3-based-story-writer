A SWIMMER (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    Henrietta Hen ran as fast as she could down the bank and stood as near  the water as she dared, cackling loudly and flapping her wings.  Her child, who was swimming in the duck-pond, seemed to have no intention  of minding her. Nor did he seem to have any intention of drowning; and as  for getting his feet wet, he acted as if he liked _that_.  "What shall I do? Oh, what shall I do?" Henrietta Hen squawked.
