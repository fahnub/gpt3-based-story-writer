And always when she  heard the creak and rattle of a wagon, as the sun rose higher and  higher, she crept into the bushes and hid until she had the road to  herself again.  If Miss Kitty hadn't been homesick she would have thought her adventure  a great lark. But somehow she couldn't get Mrs. Green's house out of her  mind.
