Really, the room was more dainty and comfortable than one might  suppose possible in a palace far beneath the surface of the sea, and  Trot was greatly delighted with her new quarters. The mermaid  attendants assisted the child to dress herself in one of the  prettiest robes, which she found to be quite dry and fitted her  comfortably.
