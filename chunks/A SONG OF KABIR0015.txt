"I knew men would follow," said Akela triumphantly. "Not for nothing  have I led the Pack."  The four cubs said nothing, but ran down hill on their bellies, melting  into the thorn and under-brush as a mole melts into a lawn.  "Where go ye, and without word?" Mowgli called.  "H'sh! We roll his skull here before mid-day!" Gray Brother answered.
