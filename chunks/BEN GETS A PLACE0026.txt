By the school they went, and the boy  looked pityingly at the black, brown, and yellow heads bobbing past the  windows as a class went up to recite; for it seemed a hard thing to the  liberty-loving lad to be shut up there so many hours on a morning like  that.  But a little breeze that was playing truant round the steps did Ben a  service without knowing it, for a sudden puff blew a torn leaf to his  feet, and seeing a picture he took it up.
