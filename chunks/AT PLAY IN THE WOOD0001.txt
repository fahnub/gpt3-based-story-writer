AT PLAY IN THE WOOD (Fable, Ages 0 to 3)    Little Red Riding-Hood was not in a hurry, and there were many things to  amuse her in the wood. She ran after the white and yellow butterflies  that danced before her, and sometimes she caught one, but she always let  it go again, for she never liked to hurt any creature.
