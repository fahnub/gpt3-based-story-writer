A QUEER BEAR (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    Grunty Pig's little eyes fell away from his mother's when she asked him  what the bear looked like--the bear that had chased him.  "Er--he was whitish, with brown spots, like Johnnie Green's dog," said  Grunty; "and--er--he had a long tail like the old horse Ebenezer's; and  he had six legs."
