Upon  which  he  continued  to  nibble    first  one  piece  then  the  other,  till  the  poor  Cats,  seeing  their  cheese    rapidly  diminishing,  entreated  to  give  himself  no  further  trouble,  but    to  deliver  to  them  what  remained.  "Not  so  fast,  I  beseech  ye,  friends,"  replied  the  Monkey;  "we  owe    justice  to  ourselves  as  well  as  to  you.    What  remains  is  due  to  me  in    right  of  my  office."
