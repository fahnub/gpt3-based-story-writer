A GAME BIRD (Fables:- Ages 3 to 5)    Mr. Grouse moved back and forth upon his log in the clearing in the  woods. And casting a withering glance at Turkey Proudfoot, he said,  "It's plain that you don't know what a game bird is. Men--and boys,  too--come into the woods with guns to hunt us. And we make game of them  by rising swiftly with a loud _whir_ and flying off before they have  time to shoot us."  Turkey Proudfoot gaped at Mr. Grouse.  "Don't they ever hit you?" he faltered.
