And he told the hot-headed younger  members of the colony to keep cool, which seems a simple thing for them  to have done, swimming about as they were in the icy water, which  flowed down from springs on the side of Blue Mountain.  "Timothy Turtle has been here before," Grandaddy Beaver announced. "I  can remember my great-grandfather's telling me about his passing two  whole weeks in our pond.
