And he vowed that he would get even with  Mr. Crow, if it took him all summer.  Meanwhile, Mr. Crow grew careless. He really thought that Farmer Green  wouldn't be able to think of any other way of keeping him out of the  cornfield. And he spent so much of his time there that he grew quite fat.  He became somewhat short-breathed, too.
