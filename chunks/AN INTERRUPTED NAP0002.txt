Meanwhile his mother slept peacefully on--or as  peacefully as anybody can who is a light sleeper and keeps one ear  always cocked to catch every stir in the forest.  She never missed her son at all until she found herself suddenly wide  awake and on her feet, ready to run. Not seeing Nimble beside her, for a  moment or two she forgot she had a child.
