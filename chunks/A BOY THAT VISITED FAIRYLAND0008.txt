KINDLY TO ELIDYR, ASKING HIM WHO HE WAS]  Soon, in front of the broad path along which they were traveling, there rose up before him a glorious palace. It had a splendid gateway, and the silver-topped towers seemed to touch the blue sky.  "What building is this?" asked the lad of his two guides.
