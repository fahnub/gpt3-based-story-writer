EBENEZER'S RECORD (Fable:- Ages 3 to 5)    The old horse Ebenezer had beaten Twinkleheels in the race to the bars.  While Johnnie Green slipped their halters on them, and they munched the  oats that he gave them, neither of them spoke. Johnnie mounted Ebenezer  bareback; and leading Twinkleheels, he turned down the lane.  "You're not as slow as I thought you were," Twinkleheels said to  Ebenezer as they drew near the barn.
