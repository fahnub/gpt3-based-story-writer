A LITTLE GENTLEMAN (Fable:- Ages 0 to 3)    All the four-footed folk in the neighborhood agreed that Dickie Deer  Mouse was well worth knowing. Throughout Pleasant Valley there was no  one else so gentle as he.  To be sure, Jasper Jay wore beautiful--perhaps even gaudy--clothes; but  his manners were so shocking that nobody would ever call him a  gentleman.
