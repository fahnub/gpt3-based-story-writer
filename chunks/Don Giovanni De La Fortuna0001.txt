Don Giovanni De La Fortuna (Fiction:- Ages 3 to 5)    Sicilianische Mahrchen  There was once a man whose name was Don Giovanni de la Fortuna, and helived in a beautiful house that his father had built, and spent a greatdeal of money. Indeed, he spent so much that very soon there was noneleft, and Don Giovanni, instead of being a rich man with everythinghe could wish for, was forced to put on the dress of a pilgrim, and towander from place to place begging his bread.
