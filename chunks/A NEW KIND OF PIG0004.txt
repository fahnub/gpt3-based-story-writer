Together Grunty and Spot stood there, silent, for a time; until at last  Grunty said, "Come along! Let's not stay here any longer. I want to see  those twelve pigs without tails."  Old dog Spot snorted.  "You _want_ to see them!" he cried. "Well, nobody's stopping you.  They're right here in front of you!"  Grunty Pig's mouth fell open--he was so astonished.
