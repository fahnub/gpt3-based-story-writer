He looked  rather cross, and we heard Jane or somebody being scolded outside by the  voice. I hope it wasn't for letting us in, but I have had doubts.  'Well,' said the clergyman, 'what is all this about?'  'You asked us to call,' Dora said, 'about your little Sunday school. We  are the Bastables of Lewisham Road.'
