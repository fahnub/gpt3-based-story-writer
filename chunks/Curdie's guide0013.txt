'Mother, mother!' he cried, 'I was forgetting.  You must take the  princess home yourself.  I must go and wake my father.'  Without a word of explanation, he rushed to the place where his father  was sleeping.  Having thoroughly roused him with what he told him he  darted out of the cottage.
