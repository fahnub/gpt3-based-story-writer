Outline: A bright summer night sees an owl in a bubble balloon sailing out of sight. The owl is hooting like Lucifer and hangs his three-cornered hat on the moon in delight.

Story so far:  This is a poem about an owl who hangs his hat on the moon.

Last chunk: A MOON SONG (Poetry:- Ages 0 to 3)      Who hung his hat on the moon?    The owl in his bubble balloon.    One bright summer night    He sailed out of sight,    And, hooting like Lucifer, hung in delight    His three-cornered hat on the moon.
