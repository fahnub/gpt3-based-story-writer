Last chunk: A BEETLE ON A BROOMSTRAW (Poetry:- Ages 0 to 3)      A robin and a wren, as they walked along one night,    Saw a big brown beetle on a broomstraw.    Said the robin to the wren: "What a pretty, pretty sight--    That big brown beetle on a broomstraw!"    So they got their plates and knives,    Their children and their wives,    And gobbled up the beetle on the broomstraw.
