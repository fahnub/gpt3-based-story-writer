Outline: When honest John Boldero says that the only way to make your candles last is to put them out, he means it literally! In this rhyming story, John explains how his wife and maids can save their candles by blowing them out each night before going to bed. By doing this, the candles will last much longer. This is a great tip for anyone who wants their candles to last longer!
